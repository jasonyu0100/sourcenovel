# Series Concept: Ignition

## Genre
Romance/Drama with Supernatural Elements — High school setting with emotion-linked superpowers. Dramatic tension with steamy undercurrents. Episodic format optimized for short-form video (TikTok/Shorts).

## Protagonist
**Rei Ashford** — Class-S ranked student at Apex Academy. The untouchable ice queen everyone wants but no one can have. Her power is perfect control through emotional suppression. Pale silver hair, ice-blue eyes, flawless composure. The crack in the ice is the sexiest thing about her.

**Kade Morrow** — Class-D "burnout" who feels too much. His power overwhelms because he can't stop feeling. Dark messy hair, amber eyes that glow gold when emotional, heat radiates visibly from his skin. The bad boy who runs hot.

## Premise
At Apex Academy, your Flame Class determines everything — social standing, dorm assignment, future prospects. Powers are emotion-linked: the stronger you feel, the stronger you burn. But desire is the most volatile emotion. Students are taught to suppress it. The highest-ranked learn control through repression. The lowest-ranked feel too much.

Cross-class relationships are forbidden because emotional entanglement destabilizes power. When Class-S prodigy Rei starts feeling something for Class-D burnout Kade, both their rankings — and the academy's hierarchy — start to crack.

## Setting
**Apex Academy** — Elite institution for emotion-powered individuals. Modern campus with class-segregated dorms, training facilities, and social spaces. Architecture reflects the hierarchy: S-Class in pristine towers, D-Class in utilitarian blocks. Temperature-controlled environments to manage power fluctuations.

## Themes
- Desire as power (and vulnerability)
- Class hierarchy and forbidden attraction
- Control vs. authenticity
- Coming of age through accepting what you feel
- The system that punishes feeling vs. the cost of suppression

## Engagement Hook
**The tension of control.** Every scene asks: will they lose control? The visual language of suppression cracking — ice melting, heat shimmering, composure breaking — is inherently charged. Cross-class attraction creates instant stakes. Power rankings make every interaction politically dangerous.

## Key Characters
- **Rei Ashford** (F) — Class-S, ice powers, emotional suppression, the untouchable beauty
- **Kade Morrow** (M) — Class-D, heat powers, feels too much, charismatic disaster
- **Senna Vale** (F) — Class-A, heat-haze powers, wants Rei, femme fatale rival
- **Theo Chen** (M) — Class-C, empathic powers, best friend who knows too much
- **Professor Vance** (M) — Faculty, broke the rules years ago, enforces them now

## Visual Style
Chainsaw Man aesthetic — simple character designs readable at thumbnail size, bold expression work, mature shading, strategic framing. Power effects as emotion metaphors (heat shimmer, frost crystals, visible temperature). Warm/cool color contrast in every charged scene.

## Direction Style
- Pacing: Balanced — tension builds, moments breathe
- Expression: Expressive and dramatic — the face sells everything
- Rhythm: Intimate — character-focused, close and personal
- Tone: Character interiority — faces and body language dominate
- Silent storytelling: Visual-dominant — silence speaks, desire shows before words

## Audio Style
Modern dramatic — electronic beats with orchestral swells. Intimate moments with minimal instrumentation building to charged crescendos. Fast starts, immediate hooks.
