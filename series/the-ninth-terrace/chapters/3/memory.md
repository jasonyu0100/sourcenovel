# Chapter 3 Memory

## Concept
A fellow Adept arrives at their quarters — someone they know, making a choice and wanting them to follow. Idris's POV as the fracture moves from background to foreground. The timeline collapses: choose now, tonight. The key moment is Idris reading through his Discipline what the visitor truly feels — and having to respond anyway.

## Sources

### General
- **Idris Vael**: Abstract Discipline (classified), constant perception that catalogs every resonance signature. Dark eyes, sharp features, mandarin collar suits. Born Ascendancy, holds Ascendancy commission. Avoids conflict out of weariness with what he's capable of. Still loves Sera.
- **Sera Kaine**: Elemental/Spatial hybrid, Tier 6-7 at this time. Luminous eyes, brunette waves. Holds Sovereignty-origin commission. Strategic mind, reads patterns. In love but furious at the system.
- **The Two Thrones**: Ascendancy (older, hierarchical, core systems) vs. Sovereignty (younger, meritocratic, frontier). The split is happening now.
- **Disciplines**: Idris's Abstract category is rarest and most dangerous. Sera's Elemental/Spatial is combat-proven. Their synergy is documented — highest bilateral output on record.

### Arc 1 Sources
- **Younger Idris**: Still vital, engaged, fighting. Eyes warmer, posture less guarded. Believes the system can hold. Approximately 28.
- **Younger Sera**: Open, laughing, reaches for him. Not yet armored. Approximately 26. Her choice of the Sovereignty will be emotional, not calculated.
- **Shared Quarters**: Officers' apartment, warm lighting, two lives layered together. His books on her desk, her jacket on his chair. Primary stage of the arc.
- **Sensory Palette**: Warm interior (amber/gold) shifting to cool (blue-white screens/broadcasts) as night progresses. The room gets smaller as the argument grows.

### Arc Definition
- **Chapter 3 Beat**: "The Fracture" — crisis becomes impossible to ignore. Orders arrive, communication channels activate. Everyone must choose. The abstract becomes concrete.
- **Pacing**: Chapter 3 accelerates. Warmth gives way to tension.
- **Avoid**: Melodrama, raised voices, exposition dumps about politics.
