# Chapter 6 Set Design: The Transit

## Locations

### Sovereignty Transport — Passenger Bay
Base: Military transport interior during FTL transit. Sharp angular design, Sovereignty aesthetic — function over form, no ornamentation. Rows of passenger seats facing each other in military configuration. Large viewport along one wall showing stars/convoy. Tactical displays mounted above seats. Cool blue-white interior lighting from overhead panels. The kind of space that exists only to move people forward.

### Vantage Station (External)
Base: Massive Sovereignty orbital city-station. Still under construction — skeletal frames reaching into void like fingers grasping toward forever. Thousands of ships in formation around it. Shipyards, research domes, infrastructure arrays. Bright against the void — a civilization building itself. The future rendered in metal and light.

## Characters

### Sera — Younger, Pre-Commandant
**Context**: This is Sera years before Chapter 1. She's just left Idris, traveling to the Sovereignty. Raw, unarmored, still becoming the Vice-Commandant.

Outfit: Elegant dark fitted attire, similar to her default but less polished. Travel-worn, slightly creased. The tech-line accents at collar and cuffs. Not yet wearing rank insignia — she's between identities.

Prompt guidance: "young woman with flowing brunette hair, dark luminous eyes, elegant dark fitted attire with tech accents, travel-worn, raw emotional state, not yet armored"

**Expression progression this chapter:**
- Opening: Raw, wounded, doing the anchoring gesture
- Mid-chapter: Guarded, clinical when discussing Idris
- End: Resolved, armored, eyes hard with conviction

---

### Reva Orin — Commandant, Strategic Command (NEW)
**Context**: Older Sovereignty officer. Sharp-featured, silver-streaked dark hair. Wears Commandant insignia with the ease of long familiarity. The kind of woman who reads synergy reports for a living. Not warm, but not cold — precise, evaluating, ultimately offering recognition rather than recruitment.

Outfit: Sovereignty military dress uniform. Dark charcoal/black with subtle blue-silver tech accents at collar and cuffs. Commandant insignia visible. Clean lines, impeccable. Worn like a second skin.

Prompt guidance: "older woman, sharp features, silver-streaked dark hair pulled back, Sovereignty military dress uniform charcoal with blue-silver accents, Commandant insignia, flat evaluating gaze, precise presence"

**Expression progression:**
- Initial: Flat evaluation, cataloguing
- Mid: Recognition, calculation
- End: Single nod of acknowledgment — teacher affirming a correct answer

## Persistent Elements

### Lighting Signature
- **Cool**: Blue-white overhead panels, sterile military lighting — dominant throughout interior
- **Warm**: Absent inside transport (contrast with Terrace chapters)
- **Vantage light**: Brilliant white/gold filling viewport at chapter's end — the future's glow

### Atmosphere
- FTL transit — warped starlight in opening beats, then stable star field
- Convoy visible through viewport — scale of military movement
- Other passengers as background elements (not focused, showing shared choice)
- Vantage reveal floods everything with light

### Sound Cues (for later video)
- Low FTL transit hum (constant)
- Soft chime of tactical displays updating
- Murmur of other passengers (very low, background)
- Dramatic swell when Vantage appears
