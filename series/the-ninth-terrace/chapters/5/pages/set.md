## Characters

### Valeria Caine — NEW CHARACTER
**Role:** Former lover, Ascendancy operative sent to retrieve Idris
**Age:** Late twenties, same generation as Idris
**Build:** Tall, confident, moves like she owns every room

**Appearance:**
- Dark hair swept back from face (contrast to Sera's flowing waves)
- Sharp features, calculating eyes
- Immaculate Ascendancy dress uniform — dark navy with gold accents
- The kind of beauty that's weaponized, not natural

**Expression range:**
- Default: Confident smile, knowing, anticipating
- Seductive: Voice dropping, eyes warming, calculated intimacy
- Cracking: Something sadder than anger, genuine for the first time

**Prompt guidance:** "beautiful woman, dark hair swept back, sharp features, calculating eyes, Ascendancy military dress uniform dark navy with gold accents, confident smile, seductive presence"

**Reference needed:** Yes — new character appearing in multiple panels

## Persistent Elements

### Lighting Signature
- **Quarters:** Cool blue-white from screens, warm amber absent
- **Corridor:** Cool blue running lights
- **Transport Dock:** Industrial overhead lighting, orange engine glow
- **Transport Interior:** Cool interior, warm city glow through window

### Atmosphere
- The quarters have lost their warmth
- Parallel cutting between locations creates tension
- The door as recurring visual motif
- Empty spaces where people used to be

### Sound Cues (for video)
- Climate system hum (quarters)
- Distant convoy sounds (quarters, corridor)
- Footsteps in empty corridor (Sera's walk)
- Dock chaos — muffled announcements, engine warming
- Transport engine hum (interior)
- The quiet click of the door (twice — Sera, then Valeria)

## Visual Motifs

### The Door
The same door through which Sera walked out, Valeria entered, Valeria exited. Same quiet click each time.

### The Collar Touch
Sera's unconscious gesture from Chapter 2 vs Valeria's deliberate version here. Same gesture, opposite meanings.

### Parallel Journeys
Idris in the quarters (temptation, refusal) while Sera walks away (departure, ascent). Neither aware of the other.
