# Chapter 1 Memory

## Short-Term Context
This is Chapter 1 — no previous chapters exist. The story opens here.

## Long-Term Context

### World State
- The unified human government is intact but fracturing. Political tensions have been building for years.
- Two ideological factions are emerging: traditionalists (future Ascendancy) and reformists (future Sovereignty).
- The fracture hasn't happened yet, but it's imminent. Military units are being quietly recalled. Loyalty tests are happening behind closed doors.
- The Ninth Terrace exists but isn't yet the neutral sanctuary it becomes — it's still part of the unified system.

### Idris Vael
- Approximately 28 years old. Senior Adept in the unified military.
- Abstract-category Discipline, classified. Known to a small circle as exceptionally dangerous.
- In a relationship with Sera Kaine. They share quarters in a military installation.
- They work together — their Discipline synergy is documented. They're devastating as a pair.
- Idris is still engaged with the world. He fights for things. He hasn't yet learned the weariness that will define his older self.
- He watches Sera with the quiet awareness of someone who knows he's lucky.

### Sera Kaine
- Approximately 26 years old. Rising Adept, Elemental/Spatial hybrid, approximately Tier 6-7.
- Brilliant, ambitious, but still open. She laughs. She reaches for him.
- She hasn't yet armored herself. The political calculation comes later — right now, she runs on talent and conviction.
- Her synergy with Idris is natural, not strategic. They complement each other.

### Relationship
- At its peak. Easy intimacy, shared danger, private silence.
- The kind of partnership built from missions and quiet evenings.
- Both are rising stars. Both are dangerous. Behind closed doors, they're soft with each other.

### Key Elements to Include
- Physical warmth and proximity — they touch easily, naturally
- Professional respect — they've just been in danger together
- The muted broadcast — political crisis pressing at the edges
- Idris's observational nature — he notices details about Sera that reveal how well he knows her
- A specific gesture or moment of intimacy that can echo later in the series

### Tone Reference
From the formula: "Open with intimacy so the reader invests before knowing what they're investing in." The warmth should feel earned, not performed. The political tension should be a whisper, not a shout.
