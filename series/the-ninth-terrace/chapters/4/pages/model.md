# Chapter 4 Model: Opposite Sides

## Scene 1: The Shared Quarters — The Argument
LOCATION: Officers' quarters — warm amber lighting bleeding to cool blue-white from screens
TIME: Night, immediately after Maren's departure

STAGING:
- Table: center of room, datapad face-down on surface
- Couch: left side of room
- Window: back wall, city lights visible below
- Door: right wall
- Desk: left wall, Idris's books stacked on it
- Chair: near desk, Sera's jacket draped over it
- Screens: back wall flanking window, emitting blue-white light

POSITIONS (start):
- Idris: standing near table CENTER-LEFT
- Sera: standing near table CENTER-RIGHT, facing Idris
- Datapad: on table between them
AXIS: Idris LEFT, Sera RIGHT

### Beat 1: Sera breaks the silence
VISUAL: Medium on Sera, eyes on the datapad
ACTION: Breaks the weighted silence that followed Maren's departure
SERA: "You're not going to open it, are you."

### Beat 2: The datapad
VISUAL: Close-up on datapad face-down on table
ACTION: The recall orders, untouched

### Beat 3: Idris observes
VISUAL: Medium close-up on Idris
ACTION: Feels her heartbeat at sixty-four — controlled, preparing

### Beat 4: His deflection
VISUAL: Medium on Idris
IDRIS: "I know what it says. The words don't need reading."

### Beat 5: The anchoring gesture
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera's fingers pressing flat against table
ACTION: The same gesture she uses before impossible operations — physical cost of not breaking

### Beat 6: Sera challenges
VISUAL: Medium on Sera
SERA: "Then what are you waiting for? Permission? An exception? The political fracture to politely reroute around our quarters?"

### Beat 7: Idris starts to defend
VISUAL: Medium on Idris
IDRIS: "The Terrace—"

### Beat 8: Sera cuts him off
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera
SERA: "Don't."
ACTION: The word lands clean

### Beat 9: Sera dismantles the defense
VISUAL: Medium on Sera, light catching her eyes
SERA: "Don't tell me about the Terrace. Neutral ground. Sovereign jurisdiction. Neither Throne has authority here. Do you actually believe a line on a treaty will protect you when both sides decide it won't?"

### Beat 10: Idris's reframe
VISUAL: Two-shot, space between them
IDRIS: "It's not about protection. It's about choosing where to stand."

### Beat 11: The accusation
VISUAL: Medium close-up on Sera
SERA: "It's about hiding. You sound like someone hiding, Idris. Not someone choosing."

### Beat 12: The silence lands
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
ACTION: No answer. There isn't an answer that wouldn't prove her point.

### Beat 13: Sera moves to window
VISUAL: Medium tracking Sera from behind
POSITION: Sera moves from table CENTER-RIGHT → window BACK-RIGHT
ACTION: Pushes off from table, crosses to window
AXIS: Idris LEFT (at table), Sera RIGHT (at window)

### Beat 14: The city below
VISUAL: Wide through window — city lights, military convoys, broadcast towers
ACTION: The fracture isn't coming. It's here.

### Beat 15: Sera at window
VISUAL: Medium on Sera, back partially to Idris, city lights behind her
SERA: "The Ascendancy is recalling by birth registry. Not request. Demand. Your origin is in their files. Every synergy report. Every mission. Every reason they want you."

### Beat 16: The files
VISUAL: Medium on Sera, turning to face him
POSITION: Sera turns at window (now facing CENTER)
SERA: "And they don't just want you. They want what we produce together. I'm in those files too."

### Beat 17: Idris acknowledges
VISUAL: Medium on Idris at table
IDRIS: "I know."

### Beat 18: Sera presses
VISUAL: Medium on Sera
SERA: "Then you know staying neutral isn't an option they're offering."

### Beat 19: The argument building
VISUAL: Wide shot of room — Idris at table LEFT, Sera at window RIGHT, distance between them
ACTION: He feels the argument building — the shape of it, the inevitability

### Beat 20: Idris counters
VISUAL: Medium on Idris, meeting her eyes
IDRIS: "Walking away doesn't fix the system. It just hands victory to the people who want it broken."

### Beat 21: His defense
VISUAL: Medium close-up on Idris
IDRIS: "The Ascendancy has rot — I'm not blind to that. But it also has institutions. Stability. People who depend on it holding together."

### Beat 22: The strike
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
IDRIS: "Abandoning them isn't principle. It's cowardice dressed as conviction."

### Beat 23: Sera goes still
VISUAL: Medium on Sera at window
ACTION: Goes still. The words land.

### Beat 24: The light shift
VISUAL: Wide shot — amber warmth bleeding toward blue-white
ACTION: The light in the room has shifted. She looks like something carved from the edge between two worlds.

### Beat 25: Sera's quiet response
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera
SERA: "Is that what you think I'm doing? Running?"

### Beat 26: Idris pushes back
VISUAL: Medium on Idris
IDRIS: "I think you're angry. I think the Ascendancy failed you in ways I can't fix, and you're channeling that into something that feels like forward motion."

### Beat 27: His assessment
VISUAL: Medium close-up on Idris
IDRIS: "The Sovereignty isn't salvation. It's just newer rot that hasn't had time to show."

### Beat 28: Sera steps toward him
VISUAL: Medium on Sera, stepping away from window
POSITION: Sera moves from window BACK-RIGHT → toward CENTER (three meters becomes two)
AXIS: Idris LEFT, Sera CENTER-RIGHT (closer now)

### Beat 29: Her counter
VISUAL: Two-shot, closer now
SERA: "The Sovereignty is building something. The Ascendancy is dying and you refuse to see it. Staying inside a collapsing structure isn't loyalty, Idris. It's denial."

### Beat 30: Her control slipping
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera
ACTION: Her heartbeat climbing — seventy-two, then seventy-six. She's not controlling it anymore.

### Beat 31: Neither is he
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
ACTION: Neither is he.

### Beat 32: Come with me
VISUAL: Medium on Sera, close enough to touch
SERA: "Come with me. Forget the commissions. Forget the files. We walk out of here tonight and we figure out the rest together."

### Beat 33: His response
VISUAL: Medium on Idris
IDRIS: "And abandon everyone I swore to protect?"

### Beat 34: Sera pushes
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera
SERA: "Protect them from what? From a government that's already fractured? From orders that will come whether you read them or not?"

### Beat 35: The final push
VISUAL: Two-shot, close enough to touch
SERA: "You can't save an institution by pretending it isn't burning."

### Beat 36: Idris looks at her
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
ACTION: Looks at her — the woman he's loved for three years. He can feel her pulse from here. Eighty-two.

### Beat 37: The core wound — her strike
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera
SERA: "If you loved me enough, you'd come with me."

### Beat 38: The words land
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
ACTION: The words land exactly where she aimed them. He feels them hit.

### Beat 39: His return strike
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
IDRIS: "If you loved me enough, you wouldn't ask me to abandon what I believe."

### Beat 40: The silence
VISUAL: Two-shot, close enough to touch, neither reaching
ACTION: The silence is worse than the argument.

### Beat 41: The impasse
VISUAL: Wide shot — both standing close, door visible RIGHT
ACTION: Sera's heartbeat steady again — forced down. His own pulse loud in his throat.

### Beat 42: The door looms
VISUAL: Medium shot, door prominent in frame behind Sera
ACTION: The door seems larger than it did an hour ago. Everything else seems smaller.

### Beat 43: Sera explains
VISUAL: Medium close-up on Sera, voice gentle
SERA: "I can't stay. Not because I don't love you. Because staying means becoming someone who waits while the world burns."

### Beat 44: Her truth
VISUAL: Close-up on Sera
SERA: "I was never going to be that person. I thought you knew that."

### Beat 45: His silence
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris
ACTION: He doesn't answer. Nothing to say that wouldn't be a lie or a wound.

### Beat 46: Three seconds
VISUAL: Two-shot, Sera waiting
ACTION: Sera waits. Three seconds — he counts them without meaning to.

### Beat 47: Sera turns
VISUAL: Medium on Sera turning away
POSITION: Sera turns, begins walking toward door
ACTION: She turns and walks to the door.

### Beat 48: She doesn't look back
VISUAL: Medium from behind Sera, walking to door
ACTION: She doesn't look back.

### Beat 49: The door closes
VISUAL: Medium on door
ACTION: The latch clicks. The door doesn't slam. It just closes, quiet and final.

### Beat 50: The room holds its breath
VISUAL: Wide shot — Idris alone, door closed
POSITION: Idris alone CENTER-LEFT, door closed RIGHT
ACTION: The room holds its breath.

### Beat 52: The books
VISUAL: Close-up on desk — his books stacked on her desk

### Beat 53: The jacket
VISUAL: Close-up on chair — her jacket still draped over his chair

### Beat 54: The datapad
VISUAL: Close-up on table — datapad still face-down, never opened

### Beat 55: Everything the same
VISUAL: Wide shot of room
ACTION: Everything looks exactly like it did an hour ago.

### Beat 56: Except
VISUAL: Medium on Idris, isolated in frame
ACTION: Except there's one fewer person in it now, and the silence has a shape.

### Beat 57: The world outside
VISUAL: Through window — convoys moving, broadcasts cycling
ACTION: Outside, the convoys kept moving. The broadcasts kept cycling. The fracture kept happening.

### Beat 58: Not counting
VISUAL: Close-up on Idris, still
ACTION: Idris stood alone in the quarters that used to be theirs, and didn't count the seconds, because counting them would make them real.
