# Chapter 2 Panels — Shared Quarters

## Narrative Analysis

**Chapter arc:** A flashback showing Sera and Idris in their shared quarters after a mission — still partners, still in love, still wearing rank insignia that hasn't yet become a dividing line. The warmth of their intimacy contrasts with the reader's knowledge of what it becomes.

**Emotional journey:** Tenderness → comfortable domesticity → first warning sign (Torren's departure) → deeper intimacy → unspoken dread → choosing silence.

**Pacing strategy:** Slow, intimate, lingering. This chapter is all about warmth and small moments. No action, only subtext. Panels should breathe. Silence is as important as dialogue.

**Visual motifs:**
- Warm amber lighting throughout (contrasts with cold present-day)
- The collar/insignia (opens the chapter, echoes Chapter 1)
- Their intertwined space (visual proof of their life together)
- The window showing the capital (external world intruding)
