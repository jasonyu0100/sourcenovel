# Sera Kaine

Vice-Commandant of Sovereignty Strategic Operations — youngest in their history. Tier 8 Void Adept approaching 9, Discipline is Geometric Manipulation: she perceives and rewrites the geometry of space itself. Distance, position, trajectory — all negotiable. She doesn't teleport so much as convince space that two points are adjacent.

When the Thrones split, she chose the Sovereignty not from ideology but from fury. The Ascendancy had failed her, failed Idris, failed everyone. The Sovereignty was building something new. She rose fast — her combination of Discipline power, strategic brilliance, and political instinct made her indispensable. The night of the Forgetting, she walked out of shared quarters and didn't look back. Idris stayed. That was the last time they spoke for years. Underneath the composure is furious — at him for leaving, at herself for caring, at the world for making her choose. Her synergy with Idris — 847% — is the highest bilateral ever recorded. His Aether shows every configuration; her Void actualizes the optimal one. They were never more dangerous than together.

A striking young woman with rich brunette hair in flowing waves. High cheekbones, dark luminous eyes. Tall, statuesque build with flawless posture — the kind that comes from discipline, not vanity. Stunning in a way that makes people forget what they were saying. Favors elegant fitted attire — futuristic, sleek, authoritative. Dark fabrics with subtle tech-line accents. Moves like someone who knows every eye in the room is on her and has decided to use that.
