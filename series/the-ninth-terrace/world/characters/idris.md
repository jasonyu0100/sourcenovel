# Idris Vael

Once one of the most feared Adepts in known space — Paragon-tier, Convergence stage, Aether Discipline. His Awareness perceives the fundamental substrate of existence: what is, what could be, what should not be. He reads threats before they exist and collapses probability until only one outcome remains. During the Border Conflicts he was legendary. When the Thrones split and he found himself opposite Sera, he surrendered his rank, his professional name, and his presence in the wider world. Came to the Ninth Terrace and stayed.

Now he runs a small Prism appraisal shop on the middle tiers and pretends to be retired. The Council of Nine know exactly who he is and consider him a strategic asset. He considers it irrelevance. Still loves Sera — doesn't talk about it, everyone who knows him can see it. When pushed past tolerance, the quiet man disappears and something precise and terrifying takes his place. His synergy rating with Sera — 847% — is the highest bilateral ever recorded. His Aether shows every configuration; her Void actualizes the optimal one.

A young man with dark, slightly unkempt hair that naturally falls well. Sharp angular features, strong jawline, dark watchful eyes that make people uncomfortable when he holds their gaze. Tall, lean build. Moves with an economy that suggests either laziness or lethality. Wears simple dark formalwear — mandarin collar suit, clean lines, no insignia or ornamentation. Handsome in a way people overlook until they really look at him.
