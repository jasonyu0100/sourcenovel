# Reva Orin

Commandant of Sovereignty Strategic Command. The officer who explains the Forgetting to Sera on the transport out — not as recruitment, but as recognition. She tells Sera: "You chose the future over someone who couldn't follow you there. That's the Sovereignty's founding story." She reads synergy reports for a living and knows exactly what an 847% bilateral means — and what it means that Sera walked away from it. Reva embodies the Sovereignty's conviction: mortality was always the cage. Every human value was shaped by scarcity and fear. Remove the cage and you discover what humans actually want when they're not terrified. She chose to forget death not to escape consequence, but to build without the weight of ending.

An older woman with sharp features and silver-streaked dark hair pulled back from her face. Wears her rank insignia with the ease of long familiarity — the kind of officer who's worn it longer than some officers have been alive. Her gaze is flat, evaluating. Not warm, not cold — precise. The kind of presence that makes you want to sit up straighter.
