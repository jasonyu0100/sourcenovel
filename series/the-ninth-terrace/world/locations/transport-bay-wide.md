# Transport Bay — Wide Interior

Where Sera met Commandant Reva Orin and heard the Sovereignty's founding story spoken aloud for the first time. Sharp angular Sovereignty design — function over form, no ornamentation, no history in the walls. Everything built new, built forward. Reva told her: "You chose the future over someone who couldn't follow you there. That's the Sovereignty's founding story." The conversation shifted from debate to recognition. The first layer of armor slid into place somewhere between these seats and the viewport.

Interior of a Sovereignty military transport passenger bay. Sharp angular design with function over form — no ornamentation, no history in the walls. Rows of passenger seats face each other in military configuration. A large viewport runs along one wall showing stars and distant convoy ships. Cool blue-white overhead lighting from recessed panels. Tactical displays mounted above the seats show convoy data. Empty seats, the space between departures. The kind of vessel that exists only to move people forward.
