# Transport Dock

Where choices become departures. The night the Thrones recalled their Adepts, the secondary port became the exit wound — officers streaming toward transports with the controlled urgency of people who've already decided but haven't yet accepted it. Maren offered Idris and Sera seats on a transport leaving tonight. Sera boarded alone. The pattern of individual departures had become a flood, and this dock was where the flood left shore.

Military secondary port in controlled chaos. Wide industrial space with multiple boarding ramps extending to waiting transports. Checkpoint stations with officers directing traffic. Overhead guidance lights casting harsh illumination. Engine glow from warming transports adds orange warmth to the otherwise cool industrial aesthetic. Functional, efficient, impersonal — the place where choices become departures.
