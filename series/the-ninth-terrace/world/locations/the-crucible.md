# The Crucible

Where the Sovereignty builds tomorrow's weapons today. A sprawling engineering complex deep inside Vantage Station where prototype armaments, ship components, and Prism-integrated technology are developed under military classification. The air hums with fabrication lasers and the whine of stress-testing rigs. Sparks cascade from overhead gantries where hull sections are welded. Holographic schematics rotate above workbenches, engineers arguing over tolerances measured in atoms. The Ascendancy inherits its technology — refines designs passed down through ten thousand years. The Sovereignty invents from scratch, breaks what doesn't work, and iterates until it does. The Crucible is where that philosophy becomes physical. Sera was assigned a tour here on her third day. She understood then what the Sovereignty was offering: not tradition, not comfort — capability.
