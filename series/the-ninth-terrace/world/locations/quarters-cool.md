# Quarters — Cool Lighting

Moments later, Valeria arrived.

The same shared quarters but with the warmth bled out. Cool blue-white light from screens dominates, amber glow faded. The room feels colder, emptier — the visual manifestation of the argument stripping away intimacy. Dark furniture, lived-in details still present, but the atmosphere has shifted from safety to distance.
