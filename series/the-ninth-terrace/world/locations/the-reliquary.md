# The Reliquary

The vault of things the Ascendancy refuses to let the universe forget. A climate-controlled chamber carved deep into the flagship's armoured core — the most protected space on any vessel in either fleet. Display cases of crystal-veined stone house artefacts from before the Forgetting: navigation instruments that guided the first colony ships, ceremonial blades carried by the Founding Hierarchs, manuscripts written in languages that only three living scholars can read. Each artefact sits in a pool of warm focused light, the surrounding darkness absolute. The Sovereignty has tried to carbon-date several pieces during diplomatic exchanges and been politely refused. The Ascendancy doesn't need scientific verification. The Reliquary exists to prove a simpler point: they were here first, they remember, and memory is power. Somewhere in the deepest alcove, behind security that predates electronic locks, sits the First Charter — the document that established the Ascendancy's claim to Meridian and, by extension, everything.

Navigation instruments, ceremonial blades, and ancient manuscripts visible in the cases. The chamber carved from dark stone with subtle relief carvings along the walls. The architecture is heavy, protective, designed to endure millennia.
