# Ascendancy Sanctum

Where bloodline becomes authority. The ruling chamber of the Ascendancy fleet — not a throne room in the crude sense, but a circular amphitheatre where the Hierarchs convene beneath a ceiling painted with the unbroken genealogy of every ruling house since the Founding. The seats are carved from the same stone as the original colony ships. The central speaking platform is a disc of polished obsidian that amplifies the speaker's voice through acoustic engineering so precise it predates the technology to explain it. No amplification systems, no holographics, no screens — the Ascendancy governs with presence, voice, and the weight of inherited legitimacy. When Hierarch Thessaly speaks from the obsidian disc, the acoustics carry her words to every seat with crystalline clarity. The Sovereignty calls this theatre. The Ascendancy calls it governance.

A circular amphitheatre inside an ancient spacecraft. Tiered stone seating rising in concentric rings around a central obsidian speaking platform. The vaulted ceiling painted with intricate genealogical murals in gold and deep crimson — family trees and lineage charts rendered as art. Warm amber light from brazier-style fixtures mounted on carved stone pillars. Rich burgundy and gold fabric draped over the upper tiers. The obsidian disc at centre gleaming with reflected firelight. Ancient stone carved with relief patterns on every surface. The atmosphere of sacred governance — ritual authority made architectural.
