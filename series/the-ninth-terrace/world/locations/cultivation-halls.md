# Cultivation Halls

The Terrace's greatest strategic asset — the reason both Thrones covet this station. Vast training chambers where Adepts refine their Disciplines under the Terrace's time dilation. A week of cultivation here equals a single day outside — the difference between a Tier 5 and a Tier 6 can be measured in months of dedicated training, and the Halls compress that timeline brutally. The chambers are individually configurable — gravity, atmosphere, ambient energy density, even the resonance frequency of the walls can be tuned to match a specific Discipline's needs. Paragons trained here. Legends were forged in these halls. And both Thrones know that whoever controls access controls the production of elite Adepts.

The Halls occupy an entire tier of the Terrace's interior — a ring of interconnected chambers ranging from intimate single-occupant pods to arena-sized spaces. The corridors between chambers are wide and functional — dark stone floors with energy conduit lines running along the walls in faint blue. Each chamber door is marked with a tier classification (the minimum Discipline tier required for safe training at that chamber's intensity). Observation galleries run above the larger chambers, separated by reinforced transparent barriers that shimmer faintly with containment fields. The ambient energy here is palpable — a low hum that advanced Adepts feel in their bones. Training artefacts line alcoves in the corridors: weighted resonance stones, Prism calibration arrays, and meditation focusing crystals. The air is dry and carries a faint ozone taste.
