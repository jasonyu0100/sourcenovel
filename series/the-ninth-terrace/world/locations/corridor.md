# Corridor

The threshold between the life they built and the fracture that ended it. Maren arrived through this corridor in field dress with no rank insignia — already made her choice. Sera walked out through it without looking back. Valeria waited in it, listening to the argument through the door, timing her entrance to the moment the wound was freshest. Three departures through the same corridor, three different futures chosen. The running lights along the floor point in one direction: away.

Military installation corridor outside officers' quarters. Functional, angular architecture with clean lines. Cool lighting contrasting with warm quarters behind closed doors. Running lights along the floor. Minimal decoration — the aesthetic of efficiency over comfort. The kind of space you pass through, not linger in.
