# Transport Interior

The vessel that carried Sera away from everything she knew. Hours after walking out of shared quarters, she sat here watching city lights recede through the window — the last view of the life she'd built with Idris before the Sovereignty swallowed her forward. The warm glow through the glass is the world she's leaving. The cool interior lighting is where she's going.

Interior of a military transport vessel. Functional seating arranged in rows, windows showing the exterior. Clean but impersonal — a space for moving between places, not being in a place. Cool interior lighting contrasts with warm city glow visible through the windows. The kind of vessel that carries you away from everything you knew.
