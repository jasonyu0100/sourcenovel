# Arc 1 Concept

## Direction
Flashback origin arc — the last night Idris and Sera spent together before the Thrones split. One night, tight focus, maximum intensity. We see them as they were — together, powerful, in love — and watch it fracture in real time.

## Timeline
Set years before the present-day story. The political fracture that creates the two Thrones is happening NOW, this night. Idris and Sera are on the same side, in the same room, until they aren't.

## Scope
A single night. No broad sweep. We drop in at the height of the crisis and ride it down.

## Tone
Intimate, tense, devastating. The reader should fall in love with what they had just in time to lose it.
