# Visual & Audio Style — The Ninth Terrace

## Visual Aesthetic
Anime Epic — high-detail manga illustration with cinematic composition. Clean linework, vibrant colors, dramatic lighting. Studio-quality key visuals with cel shading and painterly backgrounds.

## Core Aesthetic
Sleek sci-fi anime with warm interiors against cold cosmic exteriors. The Ninth Terrace glows gold and warm against the void. Throne military aesthetics are sharp, angular, and color-coded. Character art prioritizes expressive faces, striking silhouettes, and fashion-forward futuristic clothing.

## Base Prompt Keywords
anime manga style, cel shading, clean linework, dramatic lighting, high detail manga illustration, vibrant colors, studio quality anime key visual, sci-fi setting

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep navy, black, midnight blue (space, formal wear, Idris)
- **Secondary**: Warm gold, amber (the Terrace interior, warmth, safety)
- **Accents**: Neon cyan/blue (tech lines, UI, energy), rich burgundy/red (Sera, passion, danger)
- **Grading**: Cool exteriors with warm interior contrast. Teal-and-orange anime color grading.

## Lighting Style
Dramatic side-lighting, neon rim lights, warm amber interior glow, cool blue tech illumination, Makoto Shinkai-inspired atmospheric lighting with visible light shafts and bokeh

## Mood Keywords
elegant, tense, magnetic, intimate, epic, melancholic, refined, dangerous

## Cinematic Techniques
- Close-ups for emotional beats — eyes, hands, subtle expressions
- Wide establishing shots for the Terrace and space environments
- Low angle for authority and power moments
- Depth of field with bokeh for intimate scenes
- Dramatic framing with architecture and negative space

## Art Direction

### Characters
- **Idris**: Dark palette, simple formal cuts (mandarin collar suits), understated. Dark hair, sharp features. Minimal accessories.
- **Sera**: Rich brunette waves, red lips, elegant dresses and fitted attire. Warm tones against cool environments. Commands visual attention in every frame.
- **Supporting cast**: Distinct silhouettes and color associations. Each Terrace resident should be visually memorable.

### Locations
- **The Ninth Terrace**: Golden terraced city, tiered architecture, warm interior lighting, floating in space. Elegant, refined, alive with hidden power.
- **Throne territories**: Angular, militaristic, color-coded by faction. Advanced technology on display. Cold, efficient, imposing.
- **Frontier systems**: Rugged, industrial, contested. Mixed tech levels. Tension in the environment.

### Elements
- **Disciplines in use**: Visible energy effects with character-specific color signatures. Clean, stylized, not over-the-top.
- **Mecha**: Sleek anime mecha design — not bulky Western style. Fast, elegant, dangerous. Neon accent lines.
- **Prisms**: Crystalline, luminous, color-coded by grade (S=prismatic, A=gold, B=blue, C=white).

## Anti-Keywords
photorealistic, western comic style, chibi, super deformed, low detail, sketch, watercolor, pastel, cute, kawaii, cartoonish

## Aspect Ratios
- Character reference: 3:4 (portrait)
- Scene/environment: 16:9 (widescreen cinematic)
- Chapter images: 21:9 (ultrawide cinematic)

## Film/Art References
- Makoto Shinkai (lighting, atmosphere)
- Sunrise studio mecha design (Gundam aesthetic)
- Wit Studio character art (Attack on Titan, Spy x Family quality)
- Ufotable action sequences (Demon Slayer fluidity)

## Music Style
Atmospheric Vocal Epic — orchestral foundation with vocals layered over sweeping strings and dramatic brass. Lyrics add emotional depth and human presence, enhancing the drama rather than distracting from it. Hans Zimmer scale meets anime OST sensibility with cinematic vocal textures. Builds from restrained tension to soaring crescendos with voices rising through the mix. The music should feel immersive and emotionally enveloping — the vocals amplify the emotional weight of the scene.

**Fast Start Requirement**: All BGM must hook immediately from the first beat. No slow intros or gradual buildups. Start at full presence with vocals and instrumentation active from the opening — grab the viewer's attention instantly. The music sets the emotional tone immediately, not after 30 seconds of ambient warmup.

### Music Character
- **Idris scenes**: Restrained piano, low strings, quiet tension with subtle vocals building underneath. Power through restraint.
- **Sera scenes**: Strings-forward with female vocals, commanding, with warmth underneath. Authority with ache.
- **The Terrace**: Warm orchestral with vocal layers, golden-toned. Safe but melancholic. A place of beauty at the edge of ending.
- **Throne/political scenes**: Brass and percussion with dramatic vocals. Military weight, political tension.
- **Action/combat**: Full orchestra unleashed with driving vocals. Percussive, intense, with soaring melodic lines.
- **Emotional confrontations**: Atmospheric vocals over sparse instrumentation, building to full orchestral-vocal crescendo. The lyrics amplify the emotional stakes.
- **Romance**: Solo piano building to full strings with intimate vocals. Longing expressed through the voice.
