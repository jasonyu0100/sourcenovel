# Protagonists — The Ninth Terrace

## Idris

### Identity
- **Full name**: Idris Vael
- **Age**: 31 (chronological), appears late twenties due to Terrace time dilation
- **Origin**: Born in the Ascendancy, now stateless — a permanent resident of the Ninth Terrace

### Appearance
- Dark hair, slightly unkempt but naturally falls well
- Sharp angular features, strong jawline
- Dark eyes — still, watchful, aware. The kind of eyes that make people uncomfortable if he holds their gaze too long
- Tall, lean build. Moves with an economy that suggests either laziness or lethality
- Dresses in simple dark formalwear — mandarin collar suits, clean lines, no insignia or ornamentation
- Handsome in a way people overlook until they really look at him. He prefers it that way.

### Background
Once one of the most feared Adepts in known space. Held rank in the Ascendancy's military before the political fracture that created the two Thrones. His reputation was built during the Border Conflicts — a series of engagements where he demonstrated abilities that made both sides recalculate their strategic assessments.

When the Thrones split, Idris and Sera found themselves on opposite sides of an ideological and political divide. Rather than fight against her — or betray his own convictions — Idris walked away from everything. He surrendered his rank, his name (professionally), and his presence in the wider world. He came to the Ninth Terrace and stayed.

On the Terrace, he runs a small establishment on the Middle Terrace — ostensibly a Prism appraisal shop, though he rarely seems to do much business. The Council of Nine knows exactly who and what he is. They consider his presence a strategic asset. He considers it retirement.

### Discipline — Aether: Existential Perception
**Element**: Aether (Legendary) | **Tier**: Paragon (Convergence stage)

**Baseline Enhancement**: At Paragon tier, Idris's physical capabilities are extraordinary — strength, speed, and durability that would make him formidable even without his Discipline. His regeneration is measured in hours. His senses operate across spectra most humans can't perceive.

**The Awareness**: Idris perceives the Aether itself — the fundamental substrate of existence. Not just what is, but what *could be*, what *was*, and what *should not be*. His perception reads the Aether ripples caused by other Disciplines before they manifest. He knows threats before they exist.

**Active Abilities**: Can anchor targets in the Aether (preventing teleportation or dimensional escape), collapse probability until only one outcome remains, and at full power, make reality itself *certain* — air solidifies, light bends, causality stutters.

His Discipline makes violence easy. Too easy. Combined with Paragon-tier physical enhancement, he's nearly unbeatable. The Terrace gave him distance to learn how to *not see* — to let the world be uncertain again.

**Synergy with Sera**: 847% efficiency multiplier — the highest bilateral synergy ever recorded. His Aether perception shows every possible spatial configuration; her Void manipulation actualizes the optimal one. Together, they rewrite the geometry of engagements.

### Personality
- Quiet, observant, deeply intelligent
- Dry sense of humor that surfaces unexpectedly
- Genuinely kind to the people around him on the Terrace — not performing goodness, just decent
- Avoids conflict not out of fear but out of a bone-deep weariness with what he's capable of
- Still loves Sera. Doesn't talk about it. Everyone who knows him well can see it.
- When pushed past his tolerance, the shift is instant and absolute. The quiet man disappears and something precise and terrifying takes his place.

### Wants & Fears
- **Wants**: To be left alone. To stop being relevant. To let the world sort itself out without him.
- **Fears**: That he'll have to become what he was again. That Sera will see him and feel nothing. That Sera will see him and feel everything.

## Sera

### Identity
- **Full name**: Sera Kaine
- **Age**: 29 (no time dilation — she's been living in real-time)
- **Rank**: Vice-Commandant of the Sovereignty's Strategic Operations division
- **Origin**: Born in the territories that became the Sovereignty

### Appearance
- Rich brunette hair in flowing waves, often worn down
- High cheekbones, dark luminous eyes, red lips
- Tall, statuesque build. Flawless posture — the kind that comes from discipline, not vanity
- Stunning in a way that makes people forget what they were saying
- Favors elegant fitted dresses and attire — futuristic, sleek, authoritative. Dark fabrics with subtle tech-line accents.
- Moves like someone who knows every eye in the room is on her and has decided to use that.

### Background
Sera was already exceptional before the split — a fast-rising Adept with a Discipline that marked her for greatness. When the Thrones divided, she chose the Sovereignty — not out of ideology but out of fury. The Ascendancy had failed her, failed Idris, failed everyone who believed the system would hold. The Sovereignty was building something new. She would build with them.

She rose fast. Vice-Commandant by 27 — the youngest in Sovereignty history. Her combination of Discipline power, strategic brilliance, and political instinct made her indispensable. She is respected by her allies and feared by everyone else.

The Sovereignty sends her to the Ninth Terrace on a diplomatic mission with layered objectives: negotiate Terrace neutrality terms, assess its strategic vulnerability, and — unofficially — make contact with Idris. They know his value. They believe she can reach him. They're right, but not in the way they think.

### Discipline — Void: Geometric Manipulation
**Element**: Void (Uncommon) | **Tier**: 8 (approaching 9)

**Baseline Enhancement**: At Tier 8, Sera's physical capabilities are elite military grade — fast enough to blur, strong enough to breach reinforced structures, durable enough to shrug off conventional weapons. Her enhanced perception feeds directly into her Discipline.

**The Fold**: Sera perceives and manipulates the Void — the geometry of space itself. Distance, position, trajectory — all negotiable. She doesn't teleport so much as *convince* space that two points are adjacent.

**Passive Abilities**: Perfect spatial awareness within her range, trajectory reading that makes her nearly impossible to hit, and dimensional sensitivity that registers FTL transitions in her bones.

**Active Abilities**: Spatial compression for near-instantaneous transit (her Tier 8 speed multiplied by impossible geometry), barrier planes where "through" stops being a direction, coordinate locks that deny escape, and Fold Strikes that deliver blows from every angle simultaneously.

**Synergy with Idris**: 847% efficiency multiplier — the highest bilateral synergy ever recorded. His Aether perception shows every possible spatial configuration; her Void manipulation actualizes the optimal one. Together, they rewrite the geometry of engagements.

### Personality
- Brilliant, controlled, relentlessly competent
- Warm when she chooses to be — genuinely charming in social settings, which makes her more dangerous
- Underneath the composure is someone who is furious — at Idris for leaving, at herself for caring, at the world for making her choose
- Reads people the way a strategist reads a battlefield. Three moves ahead, always.
- Except with Idris. With Idris, she's raw. She hates that.
- Loyal to the people who earned it. Merciless to everyone else.

### Wants & Fears
- **Wants**: To complete her mission. To prove she doesn't need Idris. To understand why he left without fighting for them.
- **Fears**: That she still loves him. That he's moved on. That he hasn't moved on and she'll have to choose again.
