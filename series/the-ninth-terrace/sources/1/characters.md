# Arc 1 Characters

## Idris Vael (younger version)
- Age: approximately 28 (before Terrace time dilation)
- Rank: Senior Adept, Ascendancy military — exact rank classified but high enough to receive direct orders during the fracture
- Appearance: Same sharp features and dark hair, but more vital — eyes are warmer, posture less guarded. Still wears dark formalwear but with rank insignia he'll later surrender. Moves like someone who belongs in the world, not someone hiding from it.
- Discipline: Abstract category, already classified. Known to a small circle as exceptionally dangerous. Not yet at the legendary status he'll earn during the Border Conflicts (which happen after the split).
- Emotional state: In love, engaged, still fighting for things. Believes the system can hold. Doesn't yet know he's wrong.

## Sera Kaine (younger version)
- Age: approximately 26
- Rank: Rising Adept in the territories that will become the Sovereignty. Already marked for greatness but hasn't yet climbed to Vice-Commandant.
- Appearance: Same brunette waves and luminous eyes. More open in expression — she laughs, she reaches, she hasn't armored herself yet. Dresses elegantly but less deliberately than her future self. The beauty is the same; the weapon it becomes is not yet forged.
- Discipline: Elemental/Spatial hybrid, approximately Tier 6-7 at this point. Already demonstrates the synergy with Idris's abilities.
- Emotional state: In love, furious at the system, channeling heartbreak into momentum. Her choice of the Sovereignty is emotional, not calculated — the calculation comes later.
