# Chapter 3 Memory

## Concept
Senna-focused isolation chapter. Her POV as jealousy curdles into obsession. The storage room broke her certainty — two years of patient seduction undone in thirty seconds by a D-Class nobody. She schemes to reclaim Rei using Dante, but the scheming reveals how far gone she is. Psychological descent with smutty intensity. Key scene: Senna alone in her room, private altar of obsession, Thrall activating involuntarily. By the end, she decides to deploy Dante's Pulse against Kade.

Engagement: Mind-break / obsessive spiral. Edged desperation — builds toward release but denies herself. Thrall building with no outlet.

## Sources

### General
- **World**: Apex Academy — emotion-powered hierarchy. Flame Class system ranks by suppression ability. Cross-class relationships forbidden. S-Class tower (The Spire) at 62°F. D-Class Block (The Burn Ward) isolated. The Underground: rumored spaces where control is optional.
- **Protagonist**: Rei S-1 (cold/ice, emotional dampening). Kade D-4 (heat, emotional amplification). Rei's earring frosts when suppressing. Kade's eyes shift gold with emotion. Burn scars on both hands.

### Arc 1 Characters
- **Senna Vale**: A-2 ranked. Deep red wavy hair, predatory green eyes, heat-haze aura. Possessive. Two years pursuing Rei — patient, physical, relentless. Genuinely wants Rei (sympathetic). Willing to destroy Kade (dangerous). Her power: **Thrall** — addictive warmth that creates dependency through prolonged contact.
- **Jace Delacroix**: Senna's cousin/enforcer. Androgynous, auburn hair, silver rings (grounding technique). Power: **Echo** — two-way emotional mirror. Devoted to Senna in ways that blur family and something else. Echo incident with Kade left him shaken and confused.
- **Dante Reyes**: The heartbreaker. Power: **Pulse** — manipulation that makes anyone feel attraction. Senna used him before (had him seduce someone near Rei to make her jealous — it didn't work because Rei felt nothing then). A tool being used, with his own tragedy.
- **Mira Chen**: B-Class healer. Power: **Transfer** — absorbs emotional overflow. Now carrying fragments of Rei's desire. Touch-starved.
- **Nina Volkov**: Night nurse. Power: **Drain** — siphons emotions, gets high on the rush. Got a taste of Rei's suppressed desire.
- **Cross**: Faculty. Power: **Perception** — sees emotional signatures through walls. Watched Vance fall 15 years ago, didn't report. Doing it again.
- **Ivy Thornwood**: D-Class Pheromone. Green hair, golden eyes, vine tattoo. Isolated in the greenhouse. Kade's heat incinerates her pheromones — around him she's just herself.

### Arc Definition
- Arc 1: First Contact. Beat 3 (Senna's Move) is this chapter's target beat.
- Beat 3 spec: Senna escalates. Uses Thrall — addictive warmth creating dependency. Corners Rei physically. Considers deploying Dante's Pulse again, differently. The question: is Rei responding to Senna's Thrall, or to what Kade unlocked?
- Formula emphasis: Triangle Beat structure (Senna moves → Rei conflicted → Kade's presence remembered → comparison → Rei choosing). Anti-pattern: Senna must NOT be one-dimensional villain — she genuinely wants Rei.

## Key Elements

### Must Include
- **Senna's Thrall mechanics**: Addictive warmth through prolonged contact. Creates dependency. This is her escalation from patience to chemical warfare.
- **The obsession room**: Senna's private space — collected items of Rei's, memorized patterns, curated altar of two years' pursuit. This makes the obsession physical, visible.
- **Thrall activating involuntarily**: Her power betraying how far gone she is. Heat with nowhere to direct it. The body acting on what the mind won't admit.
- **The Dante plan**: Decision to deploy Dante's Pulse against Kade. Not to seduce Rei directly (failed before) but to make Kade seem common — desired by others, not special. Strategic pivot from seduction to sabotage.
- **Senna's genuine desire**: She's not just possessive — she wants Rei. The obsession is real, physical, consuming. Sympathetic even as it turns dangerous.
- **Edged denial**: Senna builds toward release but refuses it. Won't give herself the satisfaction because that means admitting she can't have the real thing. The denial fuels the scheming.
