# Chapter 3 Set Design

## Locations

### Senna's Quarters (A-Class Wing)
Base: A-Class private room — modern, comfortable, aspirational. Better than B-Class but not the austere minimalism of S-Class. Warm-toned decor, personal touches allowed. Tonight the room is sweltering — heat-haze fogs the mirror, clings to surfaces, makes the air visible. The space reads as a cage Senna is heating from the inside.

Key elements: Vanity with mirror (back wall, center) — the mirror stays fogged throughout. Drawer below vanity holds the obsession altar. Bed (right side, unmade). Door (left wall). Window (back wall, left of vanity). Uniform jacket on the floor. Warm lighting from bedside lamp — amber tint that makes everything look flushed.

## Persistent Elements

### Lighting Signature
- Warm: Bedside lamp casting amber — the room's base lighting, making everything look flushed and heated
- Heat-haze: Visible shimmer in the air throughout, thickening during Thrall activation (beats 13-16), thinning slightly after denial (beats 18-23), returning when Dante's Pulse enters (beats 28-29)
- Flash panels: Symbolic beats use bright warm flash (storage room memories) or cool blue flash (Rei's frost/collar)

### Atmosphere
- Heat-haze visible in air throughout — the room reads as tropical, suffocating
- Fogged mirror — stays fogged, Senna's reflection always blurred/distorted
- Damp surfaces — condensation on glass, on skin, on fabric
- The drawer as gravitational center — she keeps returning to it (beats 4, 17, 35)

### Power Effects
- **Thrall (Senna)**: Visible heat-haze shimmer around her body, intensifying from ambient (beats 1-12) to overwhelming (beats 13-16) to controlled (beats 18-25) to interacting with Pulse (beats 28-33) to final involuntary bloom (beat 36)
- **Pulse (Dante)**: Subtler than Thrall — a warm glow or shimmer that seems to emanate from his skin, like ambient attraction. Visible when it brushes against Senna's Thrall (beats 29, 33)
- **Combined**: When Thrall and Pulse overlap (beats 28-29), the air between them shimmers and thickens — two manipulation effects creating a charged visual zone

### Sound Cues (for later video)
- Ambient: Low hum of climate control struggling, the room too hot
- Fabric: Silk scarf being handled, shirt buttons clicking
- Breath: Heavy breathing throughout solo scenes
- Door: Click open (Dante arriving, beat 26), click shut (Dante leaving, beat 34)
- Drawer: Wood sliding open (beats 4, 35), slamming shut (beat 17)
