# Chapter 3 Concept

## Active Threads
- Rei's control shattered by Kade's touch; infirmary visit revealed her secret to Mira and Nina
- Senna sent Jace to warn Kade; Jace returned shaken from the Echo incident but didn't tell Senna what really happened
- Kade found Ivy in the greenhouse — fellow outcast, mutual immunity
- Cross watching everything, choosing silence

## Direction
Senna-focused isolation chapter. Her POV as jealousy curdles into obsession. The storage room broke something in her too — not ice, but certainty. Two years of patient seduction, undone in thirty seconds by a D-Class nobody. Her mind loops on it, replays it, fractures around it. She schemes to reclaim Rei using Dante, but the scheming itself reveals how far gone she is. Psychological descent with smutty intensity — Senna's desire for Rei is physical, consuming, and losing its leash.

## Key Scene
Senna alone in her room. The private altar of a two-year obsession — Rei's things collected, memorized, curated. She replays the storage room, but each loop distorts further. Thrall activates involuntarily, heating her skin with nowhere to direct it. She touches Rei's things and imagines Rei. The line between remembering and fantasizing dissolves. The mind-break isn't dramatic — it's quiet, intimate, a girl alone with her obsession realizing she's not in control of it anymore.

## Thread
Senna's psychological descent from confident pursuer to desperate schemer. By the end, she decides to deploy Dante's Pulse to destabilize Kade — make Rei see him as common, desired by others, not special. If Senna can't melt through with warmth, she'll burn the competition.

## Engagement
- Technique: Mind-break / obsessive spiral | Tension: Senna's control vs. her consuming need | Key Moment: Thrall activating involuntarily — her power betraying how far gone she is
- Heat: Edged desperation — Senna builds toward release but denies herself. Won't give herself the satisfaction because that means admitting she can't have the real thing. The denial fuels the scheming. Wanting without payoff. Thrall building with no outlet.

## Scope
- Location: Senna's quarters | Timeframe: Night after Chapter 2 | Focus: introspection + scheming
