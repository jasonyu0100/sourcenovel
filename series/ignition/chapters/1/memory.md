# Chapter 1 Memory

## Prior Context
None — this is the opening chapter. No previous events to reference.

## World State
- Apex Academy operates on Flame Class hierarchy
- S-Class (perfect control) at top, D-Class (burnouts) at bottom
- Cross-class relationships forbidden — causes instability events
- Rei Ashford: S-1 ranked, highest in a decade, perfect control
- Kade Morrow: D-4 ranked, held back once, doesn't play the hierarchy game

## Character States

### Rei Ashford
- At peak suppression — feels nothing, tells herself that's freedom
- Isolated by rank — no close friends, universal respect, profound emptiness
- Crystalline earring frosts when she suppresses hard
- Has never had her control flicker in years

### Kade Morrow
- Stopped trying to fix himself — if he's too much, so be it
- Burn scars on hands from years of trying to feel less
- Heat radiates from him when emotional
- Doesn't care about hierarchy — looks where he wants

### Senna Vale
- A-2 ranked, wants Rei, possessive and dangerous
- Watching for any crack in Rei's armor
- Heat-based like Kade but controlled

### Theo Chen
- C-3 ranked, Kade's friend, empath
- Feels what others feel — can sense the charge between people
- Worried about Kade's patterns

### Professor Vance
- Former S-Class who fell for C-Class
- Still wears the wedding ring
- Teaches Control Theory, watches students repeat his mistakes
