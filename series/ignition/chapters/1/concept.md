# Chapter 1 Concept

## Active Threads
- Arc opening — establish world, hierarchy, protagonists
- Beat 1 (Ice Queen) + Beat 2 (The Flicker) combined
- First contact between Rei and Kade

## Direction
Open with Kade's POV in D-Class Block — his world, his defiance, the heat that marks him as "dangerous." Then the flicker in the hall. Then forced interaction: a class, an incident, something that puts them in proximity. End with both unable to ignore what happened.

## Key Scene
The forced interaction after the flicker. Not just eye contact — actual proximity. Words exchanged. Power effects visible. Both feeling each other's temperature for the first time.

## Thread
Establishing the forbidden attraction. The hierarchy that separates them. The first crack in Rei's control.

## Engagement
- Technique: Temperature contrast, power effects as desire, the look
- Tension: Class difference makes every moment forbidden
- Key Moment: When Rei's frost retreats in Kade's presence — visible, undeniable

## Scope
- Location: D-Class Block → Main Hall → Neutral Zone (training grounds or classroom)
- Timeframe: One day — morning through the encounter
- Focus: Action/revelation — establishing characters through charged interaction
