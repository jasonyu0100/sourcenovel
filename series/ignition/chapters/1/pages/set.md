## Power Effects

### Frost/Cold (Rei)
Visual: Ice crystals forming on surfaces. Visible breath crystallizing. Blue-white glow. Temperature drop visible in environment.
Intensity levels:
- Subtle: Slight frost on earring, breath visible
- Medium: Frost spreading on nearby surfaces, air chilling
- Maximum: Full ice effect, environment frozen
Prompt guidance: "frost spreading, ice crystals forming, cold blue glow, visible breath crystallizing"

### Heat/Warmth (Kade)
Visual: Air shimmer effect like heat mirage. Warm amber glow. Surfaces warming. Radiant warmth visible.
Intensity levels:
- Subtle: Faint shimmer around him
- Medium: Clear heat-haze effect, amber tint to air
- Maximum: Full radiant heat, air visibly distorted
Prompt guidance: "heat shimmer effect, warm amber glow, air distortion, radiant warmth"

### Heat (Senna)
Visual: Similar to Kade but more aggressive, corona-like. Heat-haze forms ring around her when angry.
Intensity levels:
- Subtle: Slight shimmer
- Aggressive: Blazing corona, hair lifted by heat
Prompt guidance: "heat-haze corona, blazing aura, aggressive warmth, red-orange glow"

### Temperature War (Rei + Kade)
Visual: Where cold meets heat — visible boundary, crackling energy, frost melting, steam rising.
Prompt guidance: "temperature collision, frost meeting heat, steam and mist, crackling energy boundary, blue and amber clash"

## Persistent Elements

### Lighting Signature
- Cold scenes (Rei alone): Ice blue dominant, pale lighting, sharp shadows
- Warm scenes (Kade alone): Amber/gold dominant, soft lighting, warm shadows
- Charged scenes (together): Split lighting — blue and amber competing, temperature visible in color grading
- Triangle scenes (with Senna): Red warmth vs amber warmth vs blue cold

### Atmosphere
- S-Class spaces: Pristine, minimal, cold, isolated
- D-Class spaces: Warm, crowded, lived-in, community
- Storage room: Neutral until characters bring temperature, liminal space
- Temperature as emotion: Always visible, always telling

### Visual Motifs
- The earring: Rei's tell, shows internal state
- Open collar: Kade's rebellion against uniform conformity
- Touch: Senna's possessive vs Kade's liberating
- Glass/frost: Barriers, both physical and emotional

### Sound Cues (for video)
- S-Class corridor: Climate control hum, soft footsteps, crisp silence
- Assembly hall: Crowd murmur, distinct class segregation acoustically
- Storage room: Ambient hum, dust settling, intimate close acoustics
- Power effects: Ice crystallizing (sharp cracks), heat (low rumble/shimmer sound)
- The crack: Earring shattering — crystalline breaking sound, major moment
