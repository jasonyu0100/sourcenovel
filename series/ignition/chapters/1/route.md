# Chapter 1 Route — First Contact

## Scene 1: The Collar
**POV**: Rei | **Location**: S-Class corridor | **Time**: Morning

Senna's fingers on Rei's collar — intimate, possessive. She's been pursuing Rei for two years, using strategic warmth to melt through ice. Today she's bold: touching, leaning close, breath hot against Rei's jaw.

Rei doesn't react. Earring frosts over. She's been resisting this for two years.

Senna promises patience. Walks away like she's already won.

**Panel moments**: Fingers on collar, thumb on pulse, Senna's hungry smile, Rei exhaling frost.

---

## Scene 2: The Hall
**POV**: Kade | **Location**: Assembly | **Time**: Same morning

D-Class at the back. Kade scanning the crowd. Finds the silver-haired girl — and the redhead claiming her.

Theo warns him off. Explains the situation: Rei Ashford, S-1, untouchable. Senna Vale, A-2, obsessed pursuer.

Kade watches Senna touch Rei. Watches Rei's cold flare in resistance.

"She doesn't want it."

"Stay in your lane."

"No."

**Panel moments**: Kade's eyes finding Rei, Senna's hand on Rei's arm, Kade's dangerous smile.

---

## Scene 3: The Storage Room
**POV**: Alternating | **Location**: Behind training facility | **Time**: Later

Rei seeks empty space. Finds Kade instead.

Confrontation escalates through temperature war — his heat pushing, her cold resisting, then retreating. He sees what no one else sees: she's exhausted, empty, hunted by Senna but not wanting her.

He touches her face. Her earring cracks — ice shattering, years of suppression breaking.

Foreheads touch. Almost kiss. The moment stretches, charged and inevitable.

**Panel moments**: Heat vs cold crackling, his hand rising to her face, earring shattering with ice crystals, foreheads touching, lips almost meeting.

---

## Scene 4: The Interruption
**POV**: All three | **Location**: Storage room | **Time**: Continuous

Senna slams the door open. Sees everything.

Kade doesn't move. Asks Rei what *she* wants — not what Senna demands.

Rei steps back. Cold slams into place. She leaves.

Senna confronts Kade alone. "She's mine." He laughs in her face. "She wouldn't choose the girl who makes her feel like a trophy."

Triangle established. War declared.

**Panel moments**: Senna blazing in doorway, Kade's thumbs on Rei's cheekbones, Rei's conflicted eyes, Senna's lethal expression, Kade's laugh.

---

## Scene 5: The Window
**POV**: Rei | **Location**: S-Class quarters | **Time**: Night

Rei at her window. Palm pressed to glass — should frost, stays warm.

Senna's texts demanding answers. Rei ignores them.

She thinks about the difference: Senna has touched her a hundred times, never cracked her. Kade touched her for thirty seconds and she *broke*.

Ending: She's going to burn.

**Panel moments**: Palm on warm glass, unanswered texts glowing, staring at D-Class Block, eyes closing to remember his heat.

---

## Chapter Arc
- **Opening image**: Senna's fingers on Rei's collar — claiming
- **Closing image**: Rei's palm on warm glass — wanting
- **Transformation**: From frozen and hunted → cracked and burning

## Triangle Dynamics
- **Senna → Rei**: Possession, pursuit, "patience" that feels like siege
- **Kade → Rei**: Liberation, recognition, heat that feels like relief
- **Rei**: Caught between cage and freedom, choosing to remember the fire

## Word Count
~1,800 words

## Engagement Delivery
- Temperature as desire: frost retreating, heat winning, glass staying warm
- Physical tension: collar touches, face cupping, foreheads touching
- Triangle conflict: explicit confrontation, "she's mine" vs "she wouldn't choose you"
- Hook ending: "She's going to burn"
