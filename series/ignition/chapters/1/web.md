# Overview
Rei Ashford has spent years perfecting her cold — S-1 ranking, flawless control, perfect emptiness. Senna Vale has spent two years trying to melt through. Then Kade Morrow touches her face for thirty seconds and her ice shatters.
> The opening chapter establishes the triangle: Senna's possession, Kade's liberation, and Rei caught between cage and freedom.

## The Collar
Morning in the S-Class corridor. Senna's fingers slide up Rei's collar — slow, deliberate, possessive. Two years of pursuit condensed into one intimate touch.
> Senna's warmth has never cracked Rei's ice. She mistakes patience for progress, not realizing Rei's cold isn't melting — it's resisting.
"Let me warm you up."

### The Claim
Senna's thumb finds the pulse beneath fabric. Her heat-shimmer aura presses against Rei's frost. She leans close enough that her breath is hot against Rei's jaw.
> Every touch is a claim. Every word is a siege. Senna wants to own, not to free.
"You can't stay frozen forever, Rei. Eventually you'll want to feel something."

### The Retreat
Rei doesn't react. Her earring frosts over — ice spreading across its surface. The cold holds. Senna walks away promising patience.
> The frost is armor. The emptiness is survival. Rei has been resisting Senna's heat for two years because warmth from her feels like a cage.
"I feel plenty."

## The Hall
D-Class stands at the back of assembly. Segregated. Ignored. Kade Morrow doesn't care about hierarchy — he's scanning the crowd for silver hair.
> Kade sees social architecture where others see natural order. He recognizes performance. He recognizes resistance.

### The Sighting
He finds her — perfect posture, ice-blue eyes, the redhead beside her touching her arm. Claiming her publicly. Kade's heat spikes.
> He notices what others miss: Rei's cold flaring against Senna's touch. Not welcoming. Resisting.
"She doesn't want it."

### The Decision
Theo warns him off. S-1. Untouchable. A-2 pursuing obsessively. Stay in your lane.
> Every rule exists to keep people like Kade away from people like Rei. He's never cared about rules.
"No."

## The Storage Room
Rei seeks empty space to breathe. Finds Kade instead. D-Class heat rolling off him in waves, making her cold retreat before she even speaks.
> The confrontation that changes everything. Two powers meeting without hierarchy to buffer them.

### Temperature War
She tells him to leave. He says no. Her cold flares; his heat pushes back. The air between them crackles with power — ice and fire testing each other.
> No one says no to her. No one looks at her like he's looking — direct, unafraid, like her rank means nothing.
"I'm S-Class." / "I know."

### He Sees Her
Kade names what no one else will say: she's exhausted, empty, hiding from a woman who hunts her. Her cold keeps resisting Senna's heat because Senna wants to own, not to see.
> He reads her in minutes when others have failed for years. The ice queen isn't cold — she's frozen in self-defense.
"The girl who wants you doesn't actually see you — she just wants to own you."

### The Touch
His hand rises slowly. Hovers near her cheek. She could freeze him. Report him. Destroy him.
> The moment before contact. Choice crystallized. She could stop this.
"Make me."

### The Crack
His fingers brush her jaw. Heat blooms through her like sunrise. The earring cracks — ice shattering, crystals scattering, years of suppression fracturing in an instant.
> Senna's touched her a hundred times. Never cracked her. Kade touches her once and she breaks.
"There you are."

### Almost
Foreheads touch. Breath mingles — frost and heat. His hands cup her face. Close enough to kiss. Close enough to burn.
> The edge of everything. One more inch and there's no going back.
"I shouldn't want this." / "But you do." / "Yes."

## The Interruption
The door slams open. Senna stands in the threshold, heat-haze blazing, green eyes taking in everything — his hands on her face, the shattered earring, the destroyed cold.
> The triangle made visible. Possession confronting liberation.
"Get. Away. From her."

### The Question
Kade doesn't move. Doesn't drop his hands. He asks Rei — only Rei — what she wants. Is Senna's demand her desire?
> He gives her the choice Senna never offers. Agency instead of ownership.
"Is that what you want? You want me to go?"

### The Retreat
Rei looks at both of them. Two fires. One that cages, one that frees. She steps back. Cold slams into place. Ice queen armor restored.
> She's not ready to choose. But she felt the difference. She'll remember.

### The Confrontation
Senna faces Kade alone. Threatens destruction. Claims ownership.
> Two predators recognizing each other. One hunts through siege. One hunts through recognition.
"She's *mine*."

### The Truth
Kade laughs in her face. Names what Senna won't admit: Rei wouldn't choose the girl who makes her feel like a trophy.
> He walks out untouched. Senna stands in scattered ice crystals, hands shaking, knowing he's right.
"She wouldn't choose the girl who makes her feel like a trophy."

## The Window
Night. Rei's quarters. Palm pressed to glass that should frost over but stays warm. Senna's texts demanding answers go unanswered.
> The aftermath. Processing what broke. Deciding what to feel.

### The Difference
She thinks about the math: Senna has touched her a hundred times. Never cracked her. Kade touched her for thirty seconds and she shattered.
> The body knows what the mind won't admit. His heat felt like relief. Hers felt like siege.

### The Burn
Glass fogs beneath her palm. Warm. Wanting. She should freeze this feeling. Bury it. Be ice.
> She doesn't.
"She's going to burn."
