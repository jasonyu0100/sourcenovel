# Chapter 1: First Contact

Senna's fingers slide up Rei's collar.

Slow. Deliberate. Her thumb brushes the skin beneath, lingering where pulse meets fabric.

"You're so cold this morning." Senna's lips are close to her ear. Heat-shimmer ripples the air between them. "Let me warm you up."

Rei doesn't move. Doesn't react. The crystalline earring frosts over, ice spreading across its surface.

"We have assembly."

"After, then." Senna's hand flattens against Rei's chest, feeling for a heartbeat she won't find racing. Her green eyes narrow — hungry, frustrated. "You can't stay frozen forever, Rei. Eventually you'll want to feel something."

"I feel plenty."

"Liar." Senna's smile is sharp and wanting. She leans closer, breath hot against Rei's jaw. "But I'm patient. I've been melting you for two years. I can wait a little longer."

She steps back. Smooths her uniform. Walks toward assembly like she's already won.

Rei exhales frost.

---

D-Class stands at the back. Segregated. Ignored.

Kade Morrow doesn't care.

He leans against the wall, heat rolling off him in waves, and scans the crowd until he finds her — silver hair, perfect posture, ice-blue eyes staring at nothing.

And the redhead beside her. Touching her arm. Leaning too close.

*Claiming* her.

"Stop." Theo grabs his wrist. "Your temperature's spiking."

"Who is she?"

"Which one?"

"Silver hair."

"Rei Ashford. S-1. Untouchable." Theo's grip tightens. "The redhead is Senna Vale. A-2. She's been hunting Rei for two years."

"Hunting."

"Pursuing. Obsessed. Take your pick." Theo pulls him back from the edge of something dangerous. "Rei doesn't do relationships. Doesn't do feelings. Senna keeps trying anyway."

Kade watches Senna's hand slide up Rei's arm. Watches Rei's cold flare in response — not welcoming. *Resisting*.

"She doesn't want it," he says.

"Doesn't matter. Senna's A-Class. You're D-Class. Stay in your lane."

Kade smiles. It's not a nice smile.

"No."

---

The storage room is supposed to be empty.

Rei checked. She needs somewhere to breathe, somewhere away from Senna's hands and everyone's eyes and the performance of being perfect.

The door opens.

He's already there.

D-Class. She feels his heat before she sees him — warmth that pushes against her cold like a physical force. Dark hair. Burn scars. Amber eyes that open slowly and find hers.

"Occupied," he says.

"I don't care." She steps inside. Closes the door. "Leave."

"No."

The word hangs between them. No one says no to her. No one *looks* at her like he's looking — direct, unafraid, like her rank means nothing.

"I'm S-Class."

"I know." He pushes off the wall. Takes a step toward her. "Rei Ashford. The ice queen. Highest ranking in a decade."

Her cold flares. His heat pushes back. The air between them crackles, temperature war turning the space electric.

"Then you know I can freeze you where you stand."

"You could." Another step. Closer. "But you won't."

"You don't know what I'll do."

"I know you're hiding." His voice drops, rough and warm. "I know that redhead's been touching you all morning and your cold keeps pushing her away. I know you came here to feel something other than hunted."

Rei's breath catches. Her composure cracks — just a fracture, just a moment. But he sees it.

"There." He's close now. Close enough that his heat is winning, her frost retreating inch by inch. "That's what you look like when you stop pretending."

"I'm not—"

"You're exhausted." His hand rises slowly. Hovers near her cheek. "You're empty. And the girl who wants you doesn't actually see you — she just wants to own you."

"Stop."

"Make me."

She should. Freeze him. Report him. Destroy him for daring to stand this close, speak this way, *see* this much.

But his heat feels like relief.

And she's so tired of being cold.

"What's your name?" she whispers.

"Kade." His fingers brush her jaw. Heat blooms through her like sunrise, spreading under her skin, melting something she didn't know was frozen. "Kade Morrow."

The earring cracks.

Ice shatters from its surface — crystalline shards scattering, years of suppression fracturing all at once. Rei gasps. Her eyes flutter closed. Her lips part.

Kade's thumb traces her cheekbone. "There you are."

"I don't—" Her voice breaks. "I don't know what's happening."

"Your ice is melting." He cups her face with both hands now. Warm. So warm. "And you're letting it."

She opens her eyes. He's right there — golden eyes, parted lips, heat rolling off him in waves that make her whole body flush. Close enough to kiss. Close enough to burn.

"I shouldn't want this," she breathes.

"But you do."

"Yes."

His forehead touches hers. His breath mingles with her frost. The temperature between them is chaos — hot and cold and cracking and *alive*.

"Rei—"

The door slams open.

Senna stands in the threshold, heat-haze blazing around her like a corona. Her green eyes take in everything — Kade's hands on Rei's face, the shattered earring, the destroyed cold.

Her expression goes lethal.

"Get. Away. From her."

Kade doesn't move. His thumbs stroke Rei's cheekbones once more — gentle, deliberate, *claiming* in a way completely different from Senna's possessive touch.

"Is that what you want?" he asks Rei. Only Rei. "You want me to go?"

Rei looks at him. Looks at Senna.

Two fires. One that wants to cage her. One that wants to free her.

She steps back.

Kade's hands fall away. The cold slams back into place — ice queen armor snapping closed, frost spreading, everything frozen again.

Except she can still feel him. Everywhere he touched, burning.

"Professor Vance needs me," Rei says. Her voice is ice. Her eyes aren't.

She walks past Senna without looking at her.

Senna doesn't follow. Not yet. She steps into the room instead, heat flaring, and Kade meets her fury with a lazy smile.

"Stay away from her, burnout."

"Or what?"

"I'll destroy you." Senna's voice is soft and vicious. "I'll make sure you never get close to her again. She's *mine*."

Kade laughs. Actually laughs — low and warm and completely unafraid.

"She's not anyone's." He walks toward the door, toward Senna, close enough that their powers clash and spark. "But if she was going to be someone's? She wouldn't choose the girl who makes her feel like a trophy."

He brushes past her. Gone.

Senna stands alone in the storage room, surrounded by scattered ice crystals and the ghost of heat that doesn't belong to her.

Her hands shake.

Her power *burns*.

---

Night.

Rei presses her palm to the window glass. It should frost over. It always does.

The glass stays warm.

Her datapad buzzes. Senna.

*We need to talk.*

*He's dangerous, Rei.*

*You don't know what you're doing.*

She doesn't answer.

She stares at D-Class Block in the distance and thinks about golden eyes and burning hands and the way her ice shattered like it was *waiting* for him.

Senna has touched her a hundred times. Warmed her. Wanted her.

Rei never cracked. Not once.

Kade touched her for thirty seconds and she *broke*.

The glass fogs beneath her palm. Warm. Wanting.

She should freeze this feeling. Bury it. Be the ice queen everyone expects.

Instead, she closes her eyes and lets herself remember his heat.

*Kade Morrow.*

She's going to burn.
