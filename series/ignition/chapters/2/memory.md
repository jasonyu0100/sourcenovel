# Chapter 2 Memory

## Concept
Arc 1, Beat 2: The Fallout. The night after first contact. Rei's control is shattered and she needs the infirmary. This chapter introduces four new characters — Mira (healer who absorbs secrets), Nina (night nurse who gets high on drained emotions), Jace (Senna's enforcer sent to warn Kade), and Ivy (fellow D-Class in the greenhouse). Meanwhile, Professor Cross has been watching and choosing silence.

## Sources

### General
- **World**: Apex Academy, Flame Class hierarchy (S-D ranking), cross-class relationships forbidden, emotional powers manifest through control/loss of control
- **Protagonist (Rei)**: S-1, ice/cold powers, crystalline earring frosts when suppressing, exhausted by suppression, now cracked after Kade's touch
- **Protagonist (Kade)**: D-4, heat/Radiance powers, burn scars on hands, doesn't play the hierarchy game, all in from the start

### Arc 1
- **Characters**: Primary triangle (Rei/Kade/Senna), secondary (Theo as witness, Vance as warning)
- **Setting**: S-Class Tower (cold, clinical), D-Class Block (warm, scarred), Training Grounds (white, monitored), Neutral Zones (mixed)
- **New for Chapter 2**: Infirmary (Mira/Nina's domain), D-Class hallways (Jace confrontation), Greenhouse Wing (Ivy's isolation)

### Arc Definition
- **Beat 2 Requirements**: Infirmary scene (Mira absorbs Rei's secret, Nina witnesses), Jace warns Kade (Echo catches Radiance), Kade meets Ivy (fellow burnout), Cross watches from shadows
- **Scene Structures**: Watcher Beat (observe, witness, choose silence, complicity binds), Enforcer Beat (message delivered, Echo catches something real, both changed), Outcast Beat (powers cancel out, recognition, alliance forming)
- **Anti-Patterns**: Don't make new characters plot devices — each has their own desire

## Key Elements

### Must Include
- **Mira Chen**: Heals Rei with Transfer, absorbs fragments of desire/terror/thaw. B-Class, Theo's older sister. Soft curves, glasses, cardigan, touch-starved. Now knows the secret.
- **Nina Volkov**: Night shift, Drain power gets her high on dissipating emotions. Was on duty when Rei came in. Sedated her. Still riding the high. Platinum hair, nurse uniform too small.
- **Jace Delacroix**: Senna's B-Class cousin and enforcer. Echo power — skin contact creates two-way emotional mirror. Pretty, androgynous, silver rings on every finger for grounding. Sent to warn Kade, came back changed.
- **Ivy Thornwood**: D-Class Pheromone, isolated in greenhouse because she can't control emissions. Green hair, voluptuous, vine tattoo. Kade's heat burns away her power. Around him, she's just herself.
- **Professor Cross**: Saw the storage room encounter. Chose not to report. Perception power sees emotional signatures. Watching history repeat.

- Rei's **Null**: Still flickering, can't restore full suppression
- Kade's **Radiance**: Amplifies emotions in everyone nearby, including Jace

- Nina's **Drain**: Siphons emotions TO nothing, gets high on release
- Jace's **Echo**: Two-way mirror, both parties feel everything, escalates with contact
- Ivy's **Pheromone**: Chemical desire she can't control, except Kade burns it away
- Cross's **Perception**: Sees emotional signatures through walls
