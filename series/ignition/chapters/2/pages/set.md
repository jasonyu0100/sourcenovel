## Lighting Signatures

### Infirmary
- Base: Cool fluorescent white with blue undertones
- During Transfer: Pink/magenta warmth bleeding into Mira's space
- Contrast: Rei's cold vs absorbed warmth

### D-Class Hallway
- Base: Warm amber industrial
- Echo Effect: Auburn and amber colors tangling, neither pure
- Aftermath: Heat lingering in the air

### Greenhouse
- Base: Dark green with moonlight through glass
- Pheromone Effect: Green mist reaching outward
- Burn Effect: Amber heat incinerating green, leaving clear air

### Administrative Wing
- Base: Near darkness, minimal cold light
- Perception Effect: Colored emotional signatures visible through walls (pink, amber, green, auburn)
