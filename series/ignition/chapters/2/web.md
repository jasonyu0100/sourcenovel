# Overview
Hours after the storage room, Rei's control is shattered and she needs the infirmary. The healer who touches her absorbs her secret. Senna's enforcer confronts Kade and leaves changed. A fellow D-Class meets Kade without power games. And someone watches it all, choosing silence.
> The web of complicity grows. Four new characters enter the story, each carrying pieces of secrets that could destroy the ice queen — and none of them report what they know.

## Control Failing
Rei's temperature regulation is shot. Frost forming on her fingertips, then melting. The cracked earring hangs visible — evidence of what happened in the storage room.
> Thirty seconds of contact with Kade undid years of perfected suppression. Her body still remembers his warmth.
"Nothing feels cold enough anymore. Not since him."

### The Memory
She can't stop touching the damaged earring. Can't stop remembering how it shattered when he cupped her face.
> The physical evidence of desire — frost crystals scattered, composure cracked, control surrendered.
"I shouldn't want this. But you do. Yes."

## The Infirmary
Mira Chen takes her intake. B-Class healer, Theo's sister, invisible until you need her touch. Her power is Transfer: she absorbs emotional overflow to accelerate healing.
> The healer who feels what you feel. Every patient leaves pieces of themselves inside her.
"Your control signature is destabilized. I'll need physical contact to assess the damage."

### Transfer Activates
The moment Mira's fingers touch Rei's wrist, everything changes. Mira gasps — flooded with fragments of the storage room.
> Transfer doesn't just heal. It reveals. Every secret written in emotional residue becomes Mira's to carry.
"Amber heat. Hands cupping her face. The earring cracking, ice crystals scattering."

### The Absorption
Mira feels what Rei felt. Kade's warmth spreading through borrowed sensations. The way ice shattered not from force but from relief.
> Mira is absorbing a secret that could destroy the S-1 ranked student at Apex Academy — and she can't stop feeling it.
"The hunger beneath the frost, finally freed."

### The Recognition
Their eyes meet. Rei sees that Mira knows — the storage room, the touch, the wanting. Written in the flush spreading from chest to cheeks.
> The silence between them is loaded. Dangerous. A secret now lives in someone else's body.
"She finishes the healing with hands that shake slightly, carrying pieces of Rei's desire inside her now."

## The Night Nurse
Nina Volkov appears in the doorway. Platinum hair, uniform too small, pale blue eyes still hazy. She was on intake earlier — sedated Rei with Drain when she first arrived.
> Nina's power siphons emotions to nothing. The rush of that dissipation hits her like a drug. She got high on Rei's suppressed desire.
"She looks flushed. Breathless. Like she just finished something."

### Knowing Amusement
Nina watches Mira's flushed cheeks, the fog on her glasses. She recognizes the signs of something interesting happening.
> Two watchers now. One absorbed the secret into herself. One got high on it. Neither will report.
"Something interesting happening here."

## The Warning
Jace Delacroix finds Kade in the D-Class hallways. Pretty and dangerous — androgynous features, auburn hair, silver rings on every finger. Senna's cousin. Senna's enforcer.
> The rings are grounding technique. Without them, his Echo spirals out of control. He needs them for what he's about to do.
"My cousin sends a message."

### The Threat
Stay away from Rei, or face destruction. Ranking, future, everything — gone. Senna will burn Kade to nothing for touching what's hers.
> The devotion bleeds through every syllable. Not just following orders. Believing them.
"She will burn you to nothing for touching what's hers."

### Kade's Refusal
Kade laughs. The same laugh he gave Senna in the storage room. Low and warm and completely unafraid.
> He doesn't play the hierarchy game. Never has. Certainly won't start now.
"She's not anyone's."

## The Echo Incident
Jace's patience snaps. He grabs Kade's arm — bare skin to bare skin, no rings between them. Echo activates.
> The two-way mirror opens. Both parties feel everything the other feels. Both sensations bounce back and forth, amplifying.
"The mirror opens and they both fall in."

### Radiance Floods In
Jace feels Kade's heat, certainty, all-in devotion to Rei. It pours through the connection, overwhelming, undeniable.
> Jace came to deliver a warning. He's receiving something else entirely — desire that feels like his own.
"The all-in devotion to a silver-haired ice queen who shattered in his hands and whispered yes."

### Devotion Echoed Back
Kade feels Jace's devotion to Senna. Desperate. Complicated. Something that blurs family and want.
> The ache of serving someone who will never see you the way you need. Jace's secret, now shared.
"The way he follows Senna's orders not just from family loyalty but from something else."

### Both Changed
Jace releases him like he's been burned. Steps back, breathing hard, rings clinking as hands shake. Something changed in that hallway.
> He'll report the warning was delivered. He won't mention what happened. The heat still lingering under his skin.
"Jace doesn't have words for what."

## The Greenhouse
Kade needs space. He finds the greenhouse wing — the only place D-Class can breathe without surveillance. Isolation by design.
> They keep someone here. Someone whose emissions require special containment.
"Ventilation systems mask heat signatures."

### Ivy Thornwood
Green hair falling in waves. Half-lidded golden eyes. Vine tattoo curling up her thigh. D-Class Pheromone — isolated because she can't control what she exudes.
> Everyone who visits leaves wanting more. Everyone except the ones who are immune.
"Her power reaches for him automatically. Chemical desire, manufactured wanting."

### Powers Cancel
His heat incinerates her pheromones before they reach him. The chemical desire burns away in his Radiance. Nothing but clear air.
> For the first time, Ivy is just herself. No manipulation. No power games. Kade just is — too much heat for chemistry to take hold.
"You're immune. That's new."

### Fellow Outcasts
Two D-Class burnouts meeting in the only place either can exist. Both punished for leaking what they can't contain.
> Recognition. One outcast seeing another. Neither makes a move. But Ivy's watching with genuine interest now.
"Not desire manufactured by pheromones. Something real."

---

## The Watcher
Professor Cross stands in the shadows, watching emotional signatures through walls. She sees it all — Mira's colors shifting, Nina's drugged haze, Jace and Kade's tangling, the greenhouse meeting.
> Her power is Perception. Colors bleeding through architecture. Every secret visible to her.
"Auburn and amber tangling, Echo reflecting Radiance until neither signature is pure anymore."

### History Repeating
Fifteen years ago, Cross watched Vance fall for a C-Class. Watched their colors merge exactly like Rei and Kade's did in the storage room. She didn't report it then.
> The instability event hospitalized three people. Vance's power burned out. His career ended. She told herself never again.
"Cold blue cracking, amber flooding in, something volatile and beautiful being born."

### Choosing Silence
She's doing it again now. Watching. Not reporting. The secret binds her too.
> Everyone who knows has chosen complicity. The web grows tighter.
"Watching. Not reporting."
