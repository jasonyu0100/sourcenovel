# Chapter 2 — The Fallout

## Beat
Arc 1, Beat 2: The Fallout

## Objective
The night after first contact. Rei's control is shattered — she needs the infirmary. This chapter introduces the watchers, the enforcer, and the fellow outcast while the main triangle's tension continues to build.

## Must Introduce
- **Mira Chen** — heals Rei with Transfer, absorbs fragments of her desire and terror. Now knows the secret.
- **Nina Volkov** — night shift handoff, got a taste of Rei's suppressed heat. Watches from the doorway.
- **Jace Delacroix** — sent by Senna to warn Kade. Echo catches Radiance. Both leave the hallway changed.
- **Ivy Thornwood** — Kade retreats to the greenhouse wing. Meets a fellow D-Class. His heat burns her pheromones. For once, she's just herself.

## Must Establish
- Professor Cross observed the storage room. Chose not to report.
- Mira and Nina work opposite infirmary shifts — day and night.
- Jace's devotion to Senna is complicated by what he felt from Kade.
- Ivy and Kade recognize something in each other — fellow burnouts.

## Hooks
- Mira knows. What will she do with the secret?
- Jace came back different. What happened in that hallway?
- Ivy felt something real for the first time. Will she pursue it?
- Cross is watching. Why doesn't she report?

## Heat Level
Aftermath heat — the memory of the storage room still burning. Rei's body still warm from Kade's touch. Mira absorbing that warmth. The Echo/Radiance incident between Jace and Kade is charged in an unexpected way.

## POV Structure
- Rei (infirmary) — being healed by Mira, body still betraying her
- Kade (warning + greenhouse) — confronted by Jace, then finds Ivy
- Intercut or end with: Cross watching, choosing silence
