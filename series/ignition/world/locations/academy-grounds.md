# Academy Grounds

The neutral zone — cross-class common space where the hierarchy relaxes just enough for accidental encounters. Technically accessible to all, socially segregated by unspoken rules. Where Rei and Kade might legitimately cross paths without triggering surveillance. The campus layout itself enforces the divide: S-Class Tower rising in glass and blue light on one side, D-Class Block hunched in concrete and amber on the other, with pathways between that no one crosses without reason.

The outdoor campus of Apex Academy. Modern architecture, clean lines, perpetual overcast sky. The glass S-Class Tower visible in the distance bathed in blue. The concrete D-Class Block on the opposite side with warm amber glow. Pathways connecting class-segregated spaces. Climate-controlled but outdoors.
