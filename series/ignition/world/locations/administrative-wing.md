# Administrative Wing

Professor Cross's domain. Her Perception reads emotional signatures through walls — she sees desire as color, fear as texture, suppression as absence. From these dark corridors she watched Vance's scandal unfold fifteen years ago and said nothing. Now she watches Rei and Kade with the same intensity, reading every glance and accidental touch from positions they can't detect. She tells herself it's vigilance. Vance has noticed her distraction from the office they share. He recognizes the signs.

Faculty area of the academy. Dark corridors with minimal lighting, cold institutional architecture. Alcoves and corners provide observation positions. The darkness softens the institutional coldness but amplifies the sense of being watched.
