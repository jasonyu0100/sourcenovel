# Storage Room

Where the ice cracked. The unmonitored space behind the training facility where Kade cupped Rei's face and her frost shattered. Thirty seconds of contact undid three years of perfect control. The earring broke. She said "I shouldn't want this" and then "Yes." Professor Cross watched the entire encounter through the wall — saw Rei's cold blue signature crack and merge with Kade's amber heat. She should have reported it. She went home and couldn't sleep. This forgotten room is now the most dangerous place on campus — proof that the hierarchy can break.

Hidden space behind the training facility. Forgotten, unmonitored. Dim lighting, industrial shelving, concrete walls. The air carries residual temperature fluctuations from nearby students' power leakage. A liminal space where rules feel distant.
