# Training Grounds

The academy's classroom for emotional suppression. Cold showers, meditation chambers, endurance tests — students learn control through repeated punishment of feeling. The temperature monitors track every spike, every crack in composure. Observation galleries above let faculty watch students break in real time. This is where Rei learned to feel nothing, where Kade was told he was broken, where the system teaches that wanting is weakness.
