# The Ranking Board

The physical manifestation of the academy's hierarchy. Your number determines your life — where you live, who you eat with, who you can date, your career prospects, your social worth. Updated every Friday. Everyone checks it. Everyone pretends they don't care. Climbing is possible; falling is devastating. Cross-class relationships trigger ranking penalties. When Rei and Kade's entanglement becomes public, this board will be the weapon used against them.

Public display in the main atrium showing every student's Flame Class ranking. Updated weekly. Digital screens mounted on a central pillar. Everyone's status visible. The heart of the hierarchy — where social worth is quantified and displayed for all to see.
