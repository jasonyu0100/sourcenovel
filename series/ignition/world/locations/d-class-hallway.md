# D-Class Hallway

Where Senna's warning became something else. After discovering Rei with Kade, Senna sent her cousin Jace to deliver a threat. The confrontation escalated — Jace grabbed Kade's arm and Echo caught Radiance, bouncing heat and devotion between them until neither could tell whose feeling was whose. They don't talk about what happened in this hallway. Jace reported the warning delivered. He didn't mention the rest.

Corridor within the D-Class Block dormitory. Long concrete hallway with heat-scarred walls showing scorch marks from power surges. Warm amber industrial lighting. Metal doors spaced along the walls. Utilitarian but alive — the architecture says dangerous but real.
