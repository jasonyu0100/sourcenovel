# Assembly Hall (Back Section)

Where Kade first saw Rei across the divide. D-Class students stand in the back while S-Class sits in the front — the hierarchy made seating chart. Kade saw Senna's possessive hand on Rei's collar, saw Rei's cold flaring in resistance, and read her instantly: she doesn't want it. She's hiding. She's exhausted. This is where the spark caught — not in a private room but across a crowded hall, through the entire social structure designed to keep them apart.

The rear section of the main assembly hall where D-Class students stand during academy gatherings. No seating — they stand against the walls while higher ranks occupy the tiered seats. Concrete walls, industrial fixtures, warm amber lighting that bleeds from the D-Class environmental systems. View across the crowd toward the S-Class section in the front. The architecture makes the hierarchy visible.
