# S-Class Corridor

The architecture of almost. Long sightlines where Rei walks alone, where seeing someone at distance becomes charged with meaning. This corridor connects the Spire to the main campus — S-Class students pass through daily, observed by faculty and lower-ranked students who use different routes. Senna stalks this hallway to intercept Rei, positioning herself where contact looks accidental. After the storage room, every glance down this corridor carries the weight of what Rei is hiding.

Premium corridor connecting S-Class Tower to the main academy building. Floor-to-ceiling windows on one side, frosted glass walls on the other. Climate-controlled to perfect coolness. Dawn light filtering through, casting long shadows. The architecture itself signals privilege — clean lines, quality materials, empty and quiet compared to lower-class routes.
