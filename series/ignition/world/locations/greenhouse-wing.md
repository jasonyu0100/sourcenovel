# Greenhouse Wing

Ivy Thornwood's exile. The academy calls it isolation for safety — her Pheromone emissions ventilated through botanical filters so she can't make the campus want. In practice, it's a cage disguised as a garden. Everyone who visits leaves wanting more. Everyone except Rei, whose cold nullifies the chemistry before it reaches her — driving Ivy insane with genuine fascination. Kade is one of the few who visits freely; his heat incinerates her pheromones on contact, leaving just two burnouts being themselves. Dante comes for the only honest encounter either of them has ever had — pheromones and pulse cancel out, leaving nothing but skin.

Glass-walled botanical space. Dense tropical vegetation, humid atmosphere, green mist from ventilation systems designed to dissipate pheromone emissions. Night sky visible through glass panels. Sensual, overgrown, hidden from the academy's watchful eyes.
