# S-Class Tower

Rei's domain and her cage. The Spire represents everything the academy values — perfect control made architecture. S-1 students live here alone in minimalist rooms designed to eliminate stimulation. Rei has spent three years in climate-locked isolation, her frost blending with the building's engineered cold. The observation windows face down on every other class. After the storage room, this tower became the place she retreats to feel nothing — and the place where nothing stopped working.
