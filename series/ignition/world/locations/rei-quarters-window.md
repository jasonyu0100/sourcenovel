# Rei's Quarters (Window View)

Rei's private cage at the top of the Spire. The window faces the D-Class Block — she can see its amber glow from her bed. Before Kade, this was just another building. Now it's where he sleeps, where his heat radiates, where the person who shattered her control lives on the other side of the campus divide. The frost on her window glass should obscure the view. Some nights it doesn't form. She tells herself it's a temperature malfunction.

Rei's private room in S-Class Tower at night. Minimalist space — clean surfaces, everything in its place. Large window dominates the back wall with view of D-Class Block glowing amber in the distance. Cool blue ambient lighting from temperature control systems. The window glass should frost over from Rei's presence but tonight stays warm. A space of perfect control now contaminated by something warmer.
