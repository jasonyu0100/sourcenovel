# Senna's Quarters

Senna's private room in the A-Class Wing. Modern, comfortable, aspirational — better than B-Class but without the austere minimalism of the Spire. Personal touches allowed here, and Senna has made it hers. A vanity with a fogged mirror dominates the back wall, its drawer hiding two years of curated obsession. The room reveals what Senna hides in public: the temperature she can't control, the wanting she can't direct, the evidence of how far gone she really is. When Thrall activates involuntarily, the heat-haze presses against walls, fogs every surface, and makes the air itself taste like desire with no target.
