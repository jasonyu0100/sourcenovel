# Kade Morrow

The Class-D burnout who feels everything. Power is **Radiance** — his heat amplifies emotions in everyone nearby. When Kade feels strongly, people around him feel it too. Joy, anger, desire — his emotions radiate outward and intensify what others already feel. Unlike Senna's addictive warmth, Kade doesn't create dependency — he turns the volume up on what's already there.

**Connections:** His heat burns through Ivy's pheromones on contact — around her he's immune, and she's just herself. They've circled each other but never touched. Mira Chen has healed him after multiple spikes, absorbing fragments of his desire and loneliness — she can't stop thinking about it. Nina Volkov has tried to sedate him but his heat burns through her power. Jace Delacroix confronted him in a hallway on Senna's orders — the feedback loop caught them both, amplifying something neither expected. They don't talk about it.

Dark messy hair that looks perpetually disheveled, falling rakishly across his forehead. Amber eyes that shift toward gold when emotional — warm and inviting one moment, blazing the next. Sharp jawline, easy devastating smile that makes people forget what they were saying. Athletic build, lean muscle. Sun-warm skin with a natural flush. Uniform always modified — collar open showing collarbone, sleeves rolled up, tie loose or missing. Burn scars visible on both hands. The kind of effortless charm that draws people in despite their better judgment.
