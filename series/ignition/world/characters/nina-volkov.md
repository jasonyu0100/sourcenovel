# Nina Volkov

The academy nurse's assistant — technically C-Class but works in the infirmary alongside the medical staff. Power is **Drain** — her touch siphons emotional intensity, leaving targets calm and drowsy. Unlike Mira who *absorbs* emotions into herself to heal, Nina *drains* them to nothing — the feelings don't transfer, they dissipate. But the rush of that dissipation hits her like a drug. She doesn't feel what they felt; she feels the *release* of it leaving them. The stronger the emotion, the better the high.

**Connections:** Works opposite shifts from Mira Chen — Nina takes nights, Mira takes days. They overlap during evening handoff, and Nina has noticed how Mira looks after certain patients. She hasn't said anything. Professional courtesy among people who know too much. Nina was on duty the night after Rei's control cracked. She sedated Rei while Professor Cross watched from the doorway. The ice queen's suppressed desire hit Nina like a drug — she was high for three days. Now she understands why Cross watches Rei so closely. Kade has never needed sedation. His heat burns through her power before it takes hold. This frustrates her. She wants to know what he's feeling. Students fake spikes just to feel her touch. She knows. She doesn't stop them — their little desires are appetizers. She's waiting for the main course.

Platinum white hair cut in a sharp asymmetrical bob, longer on one side. Pale blue eyes that go hazy when she's absorbed too much. Slavic features — high cheekbones, pointed chin, full lips usually parted slightly. Curvy figure in a nurse's uniform that's slightly too small — white dress straining at the chest, hem riding up her thighs, sheer white stockings. Always looks slightly flushed, slightly breathless, like she just finished something.
