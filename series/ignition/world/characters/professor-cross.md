# Professor Elena Cross

The faculty member who sees everything and says nothing. Teaches Control Theory alongside Professor Vance. They have history — fifteen years ago, she was the one who didn't report his cross-class relationship. She watched it unfold in real-time through her perception, saw the colors of their desire bleeding into each other, and said nothing. When it ended in disaster, she told herself she'd never make that mistake again.

**Connections:** She saw Rei and Kade in the storage room. Watched the whole thing through the wall — Rei's cold blue signature cracking, Kade's amber heat flooding in, their colors merging into something volatile and beautiful. She should have reported it immediately. Instead she went home and couldn't sleep. Now she watches them in class, in the halls, reads every glance and accidental touch. She tells herself it's vigilance. She knows it's something else. Vance has noticed her distraction. He recognizes the signs.

Tall, statuesque figure that commands any room. Black hair in a severe bob that frames sharp cheekbones. Dark eyes that seem to see through you — because they do. Red lipstick, always perfect, the only color she allows herself. Tailored blazer, pencil skirt, stockings with subtle seams. Moves like a predator — deliberate, economical, aware. Perpetually cool expression that cracks only in private.
