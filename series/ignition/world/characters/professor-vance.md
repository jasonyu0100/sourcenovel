# Professor Vance

Faculty member who enforces the rules he once broke. Former S-Class whose power was **Resonant Bond** — he could form deep emotional connections that transcended class barriers. Fifteen years ago, he bonded with a C-Class student. The instability event hospitalized three bystanders. His power burned out saving her life. Now he teaches Control Theory with residual ability — enough to sense when bonds are forming, not enough to stop them.

**Connections:** Professor Cross was junior faculty when his scandal broke. She saw everything through her Perception and said nothing. They've taught together for fifteen years, circling the history between them. He's noticed her watching Rei and Kade with the same intensity she once watched him. He's tried to warn her. She doesn't listen. He called Rei into his office to deliver the cautionary tale. She went to Kade anyway. He recognizes that look. He wore it once.

Early 30s, silver-streaked black hair — premature from power strain. Sharp jawline, tired eyes that have seen too much. Fit beneath professional attire. Wedding ring he still wears despite his spouse leaving after the scandal. Authority and damage.
