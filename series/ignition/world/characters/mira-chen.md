# Mira Chen

The B-Class healer whose power is **Transfer** — she absorbs emotional overflow into herself, taking it from others to accelerate healing. Unlike her brother Theo who only senses emotions, and unlike Nina who drains them to nothing, Mira *takes them into herself*. She feels what they felt. The emotions become hers. This makes her an exceptional healer. It also means she carries pieces of everyone she's ever touched.

Theo's older sister, invisible in the hierarchy until you need her touch. Works the day shift in the infirmary opposite Nina Volkov's night shift.

**Connections:** Has healed Kade after multiple spikes — absorbed fragments of his heat, his desire, his loneliness. Can't stop thinking about it. When Rei came in after her control cracked in the storage room, Mira touched her and felt everything — the wanting, the terror, the thaw. Now she knows their secret. She hasn't told anyone, not even Theo. Dante Reyes tried his heartbeat manipulation on her once. She felt it immediately — the artificial quickening — and pulled away. He's the only person who's ever seen her angry.

Soft curves hidden beneath oversized cardigans. Warm brown eyes that darken when absorbing others' emotions. Full lips she bites when overwhelmed by absorbed feelings. Long dark hair usually in a messy bun with strands framing her face. Glasses that fog up during intense healing sessions. Modest uniform but fabric clings in ways she doesn't seem to notice. A flush spreads from her chest to her cheeks when she's absorbed too much desire. Touch-starved. Knows everyone's secrets through their skin.
