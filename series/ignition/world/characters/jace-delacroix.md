# Jace Delacroix

Senna's B-Class cousin and enforcer — ranked lower than her despite family connections because his Echo requires physical grounding to control. When Senna needs someone intimidated, interrogated, or broken — she sends Jace. His power is **Echo** — skin contact creates a two-way emotional mirror. He feels what they feel, they feel what he feels, and both sensations reflect back and forth, escalating with each pass. Unlike Kade's outward Radiance, Echo requires touch and is mutual — both parties get overwhelmed together. The silver rings on every finger are grounding technique — without them, contact spirals into confession, catharsis, or worse.

**Connections:** Hopelessly devoted to Senna in ways that blur family and something else. When she touches him to give orders, Echo makes her commands feel like desire — and she feels his devotion in return. Neither acknowledges what that means. After Senna discovered Rei with Kade, she sent Jace to deliver a warning. The confrontation escalated — Jace grabbed Kade's arm, and Echo caught Kade's Radiance, bouncing heat and devotion between them until neither could tell whose feeling was whose. They don't talk about what happened in that hallway. Jace reported the warning delivered. He didn't mention the rest.

Pretty in a sharp, dangerous way — androgynous features that blur the line between beautiful and handsome. Dark auburn hair that falls across one eye, deliberately styled. Green eyes that match Senna's. Lean, lithe build with a dancer's body, flexible and graceful. Uniform modified: fitted vest, no blazer, collar artfully undone. Moves like he's always performing.
