# Theo Chen

Class-C empath and best friend who knows too much. Power is **Resonance** — he feels what others feel, a passive receiver that can't be turned off. He doesn't absorb or affect emotions like his sister Mira — he just *knows*. When nearby emotions run too intense, his glasses fog up from the strain.

**Connections:** Mira is his older sister. He knows she's been absorbing things she shouldn't, feeling things that aren't hers. He's worried but doesn't know how to ask. He felt Kade's desire for Rei before they'd even spoken — recognized it immediately because he'd felt Senna's obsession with Rei for two years. When Rei's control finally cracked, Theo was across campus and still felt the shockwave. He's the only one who knows how much Professor Cross watches Rei and Kade in class. He can feel her attention like heat on his neck.

Soft features, perpetually messy dark hair, warm brown eyes behind stylish glasses. Compact build, cute rather than hot. Expressive face — every emotion visible. Approachable, flustered easily, genuine warmth.
