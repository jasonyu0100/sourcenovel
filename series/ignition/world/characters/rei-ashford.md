# Rei Ashford

The untouchable Class-S prodigy. Power is **Null** — her cold doesn't just suppress her own emotions, it dampens everything nearby. An aura of stillness that makes others feel less, want less, burn less. The academy calls it perfect control. Rei knows it's a cage.

**Connections:** Immune to Senna's Thrall — cold nullifies warmth before addiction can form. Two years of pursuit and Rei has felt nothing. Immune to Ivy's pheromones — they freeze before reaching her. This drives Ivy insane with genuine fascination. When Kade's heat touched her, it was the first time anyone had made her *feel* in years — his Radiance burned through her Null. Mira Chen healed her after the storage room incident, absorbing what Rei felt. Now Mira knows everything. Professor Cross watched through the wall, saw Rei's cold blue signature crack and merge with amber heat. Nina Volkov sedated her that night and got high for three days on Rei's suppressed desire.

Tall and elegant with perfect posture. Pale silver hair that falls straight past her shoulders, never out of place. Ice-blue eyes that seem to look through people. Flawless pale skin. Uniform always immaculate, buttoned to the collar. A crystalline earring on her left ear that frosts over when she suppresses. Beautiful in a way that feels distant, unreachable — the crack in the ice is everything.
