# Ivy Thornwood

The D-Class temptress whose power is **Pheromone** — she exudes chemical desire that makes everyone around her want. Can't control it at all. The academy keeps her isolated in the greenhouse wing where her "emissions" are ventilated, marking her as dangerous despite no violent incidents. Another burnout like Kade, punished for leaking what she can't contain. Everyone who visits leaves wanting more. Everyone except Rei.

**Connections:** Rei Ashford is immune. Cold nullifies chemistry — Ivy's pheromones freeze before they reach her. This drives Ivy insane. She's never met anyone who could resist, and now she's obsessed with the ice queen. She engineers encounters, finds excuses to be where Rei will pass, just to feel the frustration of being *unwanted* for once. It's the most genuine attraction she's ever experienced. Kade is the only other D-Class student she knows — fellow burnouts, both punished for leaking. His heat incinerates her pheromones on contact. Around him she's just herself, no chemical advantage. They've circled each other in the way outcasts do, recognizing something familiar. Neither has made a move. Dante Reyes visits the greenhouse sometimes. They've hooked up — pheromones and pulse manipulation cancel each other out, leaving just two people who are used to having all the power suddenly having none.

Vibrant emerald green hair that falls in waves past her shoulders. Half-lidded golden eyes. Full pouty lips always curved in amusement. Voluptuous figure she doesn't bother hiding — crop top uniform showing midriff, skirt riding high. Vine-like tattoo curling up her thigh. Moves with lazy sensuality, always touching herself absently — playing with her hair, hand on her hip, fingers trailing her collarbone.
