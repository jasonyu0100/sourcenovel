# Rei's Earring

Inherited from her mother — the only personal touch Rei allows herself. In a family that viewed emotional expression as failure, this earring is the one thing that betrays her. It frosts over when she suppresses, ice patterns spreading across the crystal surface. When her control cracks, the frost shatters into crystalline shards. The earring broke when Kade cupped her face in the storage room — the first physical proof that her ice could break. Senna saw the broken earring and knew immediately what it meant. Two years of patient pursuit, and a D-Class burnout undid it all in thirty seconds. The earring is Rei's only tell — the crack in the ice that everyone is watching for.
