# The Obsession Drawer

The drawer beneath Senna's vanity — a private altar to two years of pursuit. Contains curated relics of Rei: a pale blue silk scarf still holding traces of frost in its weave, a pen with a bitten cap (the only evidence Rei has ever been less than perfect), and a glossy photograph from the rankings ceremony showing Rei at the podium with Senna barely visible at the frame's edge. Each item is arranged carefully, lovingly. Senna's heat leaves marks on everything she touches — fingerprints burned into the photograph's glossy surface, frost melting off the scarf on contact. The drawer is a gravitational center she keeps returning to: opening it like a wound, slamming it shut in denial, opening it again because the compulsion is stronger than strategy.
