# Visual & Audio Style — Ignition

## Visual Aesthetic
Anime Modern — Chainsaw Man / Jujutsu Kaisen influence. Simple character designs with maximum expressiveness, bold linework, mature shading, clean compositions. Readable at thumbnail size, impactful at full resolution.

## Core Aesthetic
High school drama with supernatural heat. Modern architecture, clean uniforms, temperature-coded environments. Visual contrast between cold control (blues, whites, sharp) and hot desire (oranges, reds, soft). Characters are attractive, designs are simple, expressions carry everything.

## Base Prompt Keywords
anime style, modern anime aesthetic, clean linework, bold expressions, mature shading, simple character design, dramatic lighting, high contrast, cel shading, Chainsaw Man influence, Jujutsu Kaisen style

## Color Palette
- **Cold (Control)**: Ice blue, silver, white, pale violet — Rei's palette
- **Hot (Desire)**: Amber, gold, orange, deep red — Kade's palette
- **Neutral (Academy)**: Dark navy, charcoal, muted grays — uniforms, architecture
- **Accent**: Pink/magenta for charged romantic moments
- **Grading**: Cool-to-warm transitions as tension builds. Temperature tells the story.

## Lighting Style
Dramatic side-lighting, bold rim lights, temperature-coded illumination. Cool blue for control scenes, warm amber for desire scenes. Split lighting (half-face cold, half-face warm) for moments of internal conflict. Visible heat shimmer as an effect. Frost crystals catching light.

## Mood Keywords
charged, tense, intimate, forbidden, magnetic, volatile, explicit, cracking, smoldering, electric, possessive, surrendering, consuming, burning

## Cinematic Techniques
- **Close-ups for desire** — Eyes darkening, lips parting, hands on skin, flushed expressions
- **Two-shots for contact** — Characters in frame together, touching or about to touch
- **Temperature contrast** — Visual warmth/coolness as desire gauge
- **Power effects as foreplay** — Heat shimmer, frost melting on skin, temperature spiraling
- **Body framing** — Hands, necks, exposed skin, physical contact points
- **Triangle compositions** — Senna/Rei/Kade positioning, who Rei turns toward
- **Intimacy escalation** — Each chapter pushes framing closer, more explicit, more physical

## Art Direction

### Characters
- **Rei Ashford**: Pale silver hair (long, straight), ice-blue eyes, pale skin, tall/elegant. Uniform always perfect, buttoned high. Expression typically neutral — cracks are the storytelling.
- **Kade Morrow**: Dark messy hair, amber/gold eyes, warm skin tone, athletic build. Uniform always disheveled — loose collar, rolled sleeves. Expression open, readable, intense.
- **Senna Vale**: Deep red wavy hair, green eyes, curves weaponized. Uniform tailored to flatter. Heat-haze effect around her.
- **Theo Chen**: Soft features, dark hair, glasses, compact build. Expressive face, blushes easily.
- **Professor Vance**: Early 30s, silver-streaked black hair, tired sharp eyes, wedding ring visible.

### Locations
- **S-Class Tower**: Glass and steel, minimalist, cold blue lighting, frost on windows
- **D-Class Block**: Concrete, heat-scarred walls, warm orange lighting, industrial
- **Training Grounds**: Clinical white, temperature monitors, observation galleries
- **Academy Grounds**: Modern campus, clean lines, class-segregated spaces

### Power Effects
- **Ice/Cold**: Frost crystals forming, visible breath, blue glow, temperature drop
- **Heat/Flame**: Air shimmer, warm glow, surfaces warming, amber/orange tint
- **Control cracking**: Mixed effects — frost melting, heat breaking through cold
- **Desire**: Pink/magenta tint, both power effects destabilizing

## Music Style
Electronic Drama — Modern beats with synth layers, building tension. Fast hooks, immediate presence. Intimate moments with minimal instrumentation swelling to charged crescendos. Dark pop influence with orchestral elements for climax moments.

**Fast Start Requirement**: All BGM must hook immediately from the first beat. No slow intros or gradual buildups. Start with presence — grab attention instantly.

### Music Character
- **Rei scenes**: Cold electronic, minimal, precise. Synth pads and distant beats. Warmth creeps in when she's near Kade.
- **Kade scenes**: Warmer electronic, more present bass, slight distortion. Heat in the sound.
- **Heat/Romance**: Building beats, layered synths, rising intensity. Sensual bass, breathy pads. The drop is the payoff — and it delivers.
- **Triangle tension**: Competing motifs — Senna's aggressive heat vs. Kade's warm pull. Discordant when they clash.
- **Intimacy scenes**: Stripped back, intimate production. Bass and breath. Building to overwhelming.
- **Climax moments**: Full electronic-orchestral hybrid. All restraint dropped. Cathartic release.
