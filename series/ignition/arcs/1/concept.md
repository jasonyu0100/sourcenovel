# Arc 1 Concept — First Contact

## Direction
Opening arc that ignites fast. Senna has been pursuing Rei for two years — patient, possessive, physical. Then Kade Morrow touches Rei's face for thirty seconds and her ice shatters. The love triangle erupts immediately, pulling in watchers, enforcers, and fellow outcasts. Heat escalates from first contact through explicit physical intimacy. Power effects are foreplay. Every chapter delivers.

## Focus
- **Primary POV**: Rei Ashford — the ice queen discovering what desire actually feels like
- **Catalyst**: Kade Morrow — the burnout who sees through her and makes her burn
- **Rival**: Senna Vale — possessive, sexual, dangerous, two years invested in Rei
- **Witness**: Theo Chen — empathic best friend who feels the disaster coming
- **Enforcer**: Jace Delacroix — Senna's devoted cousin, sent to warn Kade, came back changed
- **Watchers**: Cross sees through walls, Mira absorbs secrets through touch, Nina gets high on what she drains
- **Fellow Outcast**: Ivy Thornwood — D-Class pheromone, the only other burnout, recognizes Kade

## Tone
Explicit romantic tension. Volcanic desire under cracking ice. The love triangle creates constant pressure — Senna's possessive heat vs. Kade's liberating warmth vs. Rei's awakening hunger. Everyone is watching. No one is reporting. The complicity binds them all. Every interaction is charged, physical, and escalating.

## Central Conflict
Rei has spent years perfecting control. Kade destroys it in thirty seconds. Senna has spent two years trying to claim what Kade takes without trying. Rei must choose between the safety of ice and the terrifying freedom of burning — while everyone around her holds pieces of her secret.

## Heat Level
Explicit. Chapter 1 opens with Senna's hands on Rei's body and ends with Kade cupping Rei's face, her earring shattering, and an almost-kiss. Heat escalates from there — each chapter pushes further into physical intimacy, with power effects amplifying every touch. Characters acknowledge desire openly: "I shouldn't want this." "But you do." "Yes."

## New Character Introductions
- **Chapter 2**: Mira (heals Rei, absorbs her secret), Nina (night shift witness), Jace (sent to warn Kade), Ivy (fellow D-Class)
- **Chapter 3**: Dante (Senna's past tool, potential weapon)
- **Throughout**: Cross (watching from shadows, not reporting)
