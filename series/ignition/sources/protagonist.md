# Protagonists — Ignition

## Rei Ashford

### Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Rei Ashford
- **Age**: 17
- **Year**: Third Year
- **Flame Class**: S-1 (highest ranking in a decade)
- **Power Manifestation**: Cold/Ice — temperature suppression, frost generation, emotional dampening field

### Appearance
Tall and elegant with perfect posture — the kind that's trained, not natural. Pale silver hair that falls straight past her shoulders, never out of place. Ice-blue eyes that seem to look through people rather than at them. Pale, flawless skin that flushes easily when her control slips — one of her few tells.

Always impeccably dressed. Uniform buttoned to the collar, blazer pressed, everything precisely where it should be. The only personal touch: a crystalline earring (left ear) inherited from her mother. It frosts over when she's suppressing hard.

### Background
Born to two S-Class parents who viewed emotional expression as failure. Her childhood was training: meditation, suppression exercises, the cold clarity of controlled rooms. She never threw a tantrum. She never cried in public. She learned that love was something you felt quietly, privately, and preferably not at all.

Her power manifested at thirteen during her grandmother's funeral. She didn't cry. The room temperature dropped thirty degrees. Her parents were proud.

At Apex Academy, she's achieved everything expected: top ranking, perfect control, universal respect. She's also profoundly isolated. No one touches the ice queen. No one gets close enough to see that under the frost, something is burning.

### Personality
- Surface: Composed, distant, precise. Speaks in measured sentences. Maintains perfect control in every situation.
- Interior: Exhausted by suppression. Hungry for something she can't name. Terrified of wanting.
- With others: Polite but unreachable. Known for being fair but cold. Has no close friends — friendship requires feeling.

### Powers
Her cold isn't just temperature — it's emotional dampening. When Rei suppresses, nearby people feel calmer, less passionate, less alive. She's been told this is a gift. It feels like a curse.

At full suppression, she can frost surfaces, drop room temperature significantly, and project an aura of stillness that makes others uncomfortable. The cost: she feels nothing. Or nothing she'll admit.

When control cracks, the frost shatters. Temperature swings. Her eyes brighten from pale blue to crystalline. The composure breaks and everything she's been holding back threatens to flood out.

### Wants and Fears
- **Wants**: To feel something without losing everything. To be wanted for herself, not her rank. To know what it's like to burn.
- **Fears**: Losing control publicly. Becoming her parents' disappointment. Feeling so much she can't stop.

### Senna's Claim
Senna Vale has been pursuing Rei for two years — patient, physical, possessive. Hands on her collar, breath on her jaw, heat pressing against her cold. Rei's frost pushes back every time. Senna reads this as playing hard to get. It isn't. Rei genuinely doesn't feel it — until Kade makes her feel everything.

### The Crack
Kade Morrow touches her face for thirty seconds and her ice shatters. The earring breaks. Her composure cracks. She admits desire for the first time: "I shouldn't want this." / "But you do." / "Yes." The tragedy for Senna: two years of dedicated pursuit, and a D-Class burnout undoes it all in a single encounter. Rei's arc is discovering that wanting isn't weakness — it's the first real thing she's felt.

## Kade Morrow

### Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Kade Morrow
- **Age**: 18
- **Year**: Third Year (held back once)
- **Flame Class**: D-4
- **Power Manifestation**: Heat — temperature elevation, radiant warmth, emotional amplification

### Appearance
Dark hair that looks perpetually disheveled — like he just woke up or just finished something. Amber eyes that shift toward gold when his emotions run hot. Athletic build, lean muscle, the kind of body that moves comfortably. Sun-warm skin with a perpetual flush of heat.

Uniform always modified: collar open, sleeves rolled up, tie loosened or missing entirely. Burn scars visible on both hands — old, healed, deliberately placed. He doesn't hide them.

### Background
Born to a single mother who loved him fiercely and worried constantly. His power manifested at eleven when his mom got sick — he stayed by her hospital bed for three days, and by the end, the machines were overheating. She recovered. He was classified D-Class immediately.

At Apex Academy, he's been told he's broken, dangerous, too much. He tried to believe them for years. The scars on his hands are from the times he tried to feel less — burning himself to create pain that might dampen everything else. It didn't work.

Eventually, he stopped trying to fix himself. If he's too much, so be it. At least he's real.

### Personality
- Surface: Confident, slightly amused, deliberately provocative. Doesn't play the hierarchy game because he has nothing to lose.
- Interior: Lonely. Angry at a system that punishes feeling. Protective of anyone getting crushed by the same machine.
- With others: Warm, present, genuine. Makes people uncomfortable because he says what he means and looks where he wants.

### Powers
His heat isn't just temperature — it's emotional amplification. When Kade feels strongly, everyone nearby feels it too. Joy, anger, desire — his emotions radiate. He's been told this is dangerous. He thinks it's honest.

At full intensity, air shimmers around him. Surfaces warm to the touch. People near him feel their own emotions intensify. The effect is overwhelming and, for some, intoxicating.

When suppressing (which he rarely does), the heat pulls inward. His skin cools. He becomes quieter, smaller, less himself. It costs him something every time.

### Wants and Fears
- **Wants**: To be seen as something other than a threat. To find someone who isn't afraid of how much he feels. To matter to someone who matters.
- **Fears**: Hurting someone because he couldn't hold back. Being proven right — that he really is too much. Never finding anyone who can match his intensity.

### The Spark
He sees Rei across the assembly hall — sees Senna's possessive hand on her, sees Rei's cold flaring in resistance. He reads her instantly: she doesn't want it. She's hiding. She's exhausted.

In the storage room, he doesn't hesitate. He tells her what he sees: that the girl who wants her doesn't actually see her. He cups her face. Her ice shatters. And for the first time, someone matches his intensity — not with heat, but with the flood of everything she's been holding back.

He doesn't look away. He doesn't let go. And when Senna threatens to destroy him, he laughs — because Rei chose him, and that's worth any cost.
