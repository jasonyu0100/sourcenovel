# Arc 1 Characters

## Primary

### Rei Ashford
S-1 ranked. The ice queen. Perfect control masking profound emptiness. Silver hair, ice-blue eyes, crystalline earring that frosts when she suppresses. Every surface reads cold — until Kade touches her.

**Arc 1 State**: Control shatters in Chapter 1. Kade cups her face, earring breaks, she admits desire ("I shouldn't want this" / "Yes"). The arc follows her choosing desire over control — increasingly explicit physical escalation with Kade while Senna fights to hold her claim.

### Kade Morrow
D-4 ranked. The burnout who stopped trying to be less. Dark messy hair, amber eyes that shift gold with emotion, burn scars on both hands. Heat radiates from him. He looks where he wants, touches where invited, says what he means.

**Arc 1 State**: Sees through Rei's ice in their first encounter. Reads her completely — that Senna's touch is unwanted, that Rei is hiding and exhausted. Cups her face, undoes years of control in thirty seconds. All in from the start. Knows the system will try to destroy him. Doesn't care.

## Secondary

### Senna Vale
A-2 ranked. Wants Rei and will destroy anyone in her way. Deep red wavy hair, predatory green eyes, curves weaponized. Heat-haze effect around her. Possessive. Dangerous. Two years invested in seducing Rei — patient, physical, relentless.

**Arc 1 State**: Chapter 1 shatters her patience. Walks in on Kade touching Rei's face, sees the broken earring, declares war. Escalates from seduction to aggression — she knows Rei's body, she knows Rei's tells, and she'll use everything to keep her claim. Genuinely wants Rei, which makes her sympathetic. Willing to destroy Kade, which makes her dangerous.

### Theo Chen
C-3 ranked. Kade's closest friend. Soft-featured, glasses, empathic sensitivity that makes him C-Class despite decent control. Feels what others feel — including the desire between Rei and Kade. Can't unsee it.

**Arc 1 State**: Terrified. He can feel what Kade's feeling and it's overwhelming. He knows the academy will punish cross-class entanglement. He warns Kade. Kade doesn't listen.

### Professor Vance
Faculty. Control Theory instructor. Former S-Class who fell for a C-Class fifteen years ago. The instability event hospitalized three people. His wife left. Still wears the ring.

**Arc 1 State**: Sees the pattern forming between Rei and Kade. Intervenes — not to enforce rules, but to warn from experience. His story is the cautionary tale. They hear him. They go to each other anyway.

## Background

### Other S-Class Students
Rei's peers who maintain distance as performance. Watch her for cracks. Would report any slip.

### D-Class Block
Kade's community. Bonded through marginalization. They'd protect him from academy consequences but can't protect him from an S-Class.
