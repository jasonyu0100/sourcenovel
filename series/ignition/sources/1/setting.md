# Arc 1 Setting

## Primary Locations

### S-Class Tower (The Spire)
Rei's domain. Glass and steel rising above campus. Climate-locked at 62°F. Individual rooms with observation-level windows. Minimalist everything — white walls, clean lines, controlled air. The message: you've risen above feeling.

**Sensory Palette**: Cold air, subtle hum of climate control, frost on windows, blue-tinted lighting, silence as presence.

### D-Class Block (The Burn Ward)
Kade's territory. Concrete and scorch marks. Heat-resistant surfaces scarred by past incidents. Utilitarian, deliberately uncomfortable. Isolated from main campus. The system's message: you're dangerous.

**Sensory Palette**: Warm air, ambient heat, orange lighting, industrial sounds, community in defiance.

### The Ranking Board
Main atrium. Massive display updated every Friday. Every student's class and rank visible. People check it constantly, pretend they don't care. The physical manifestation of hierarchy.

**Sensory Palette**: Crowd noise, anxious energy, blue glow of the display, the hush when numbers change.

### The Neutral Zone
Cross-class common spaces. Cafeteria, library, outdoor grounds. Technically accessible to all. Socially segregated by unspoken rules. Where Rei and Kade might legitimately cross paths.

**Sensory Palette**: Transitional temperatures, mixed lighting, the tension of classes mixing, watchful eyes.

### Training Grounds
Clinical white facilities. Temperature monitors everywhere. Where students learn suppression through repetition. Cold showers, meditation chambers, endurance tests. The system's classroom.

**Sensory Palette**: Antiseptic clean, white surfaces, monitored silence, the strain of controlled power.

## Transitional Spaces

### The Hall
Where the first flicker happens. Crowded between classes. Bodies in motion. The moment of eye contact in transit. Neither seeking, both finding.

### Corridors
Long sightlines. Seeing each other at distance. The charged moment of approach, the deflection of passing. Architecture of almost.

## Environmental Details

### Temperature as Narrative
Rooms respond to powerful students. When Rei enters, temperature drops. When Kade lingers, surfaces warm. When they're together, the environment becomes unstable — frost meeting heat, systems straining.

### Visual Class Markers
S-Class: pristine uniforms, minimal jewelry, controlled posture
D-Class: modified uniforms, visible wear, relaxed body language

### Surveillance
Cameras, monitors, faculty patrols. The academy watches. Privacy is rare. Unobserved moments are precious.
